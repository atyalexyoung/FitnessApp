## Form Cues
1. Start bar just above knees.  
2. Pull explosively, shrug, extend hips.  
3. Catch bar overhead in squat or partial squat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arm-dominant pull  
- Weak hip extension  
- Bar away from body  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Power hang snatch  

**Harder:**  
- Pause above knees  
- Full snatch depth  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 2–4 reps  
- Explosive power or Olympic lifting training  

## Benefits
- Upper back and trap development  
- Hip extension power  
- Explosive coordination
