## Form Cues
1. Feet hip-width, bar on floor.  
2. Pull explosively, shrug and extend hips.  
3. Catch bar overhead in deep squat, stand tall.  

## Common Mistakes
- Early arm pull  
- Bar too far from body  
- Weak squat catch  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Power snatch  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Full snatch with heavy load  
- Pause snatch  

## Programming Options
- 3–6 sets × 1–3 reps  
- Olympic lifting or explosive power training  

## Benefits
- Explosive full-body strength  
- Hip, shoulder, and trap development  
- Coordination and mobility
