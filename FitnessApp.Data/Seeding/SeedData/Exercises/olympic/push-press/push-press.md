## Form Cues
1. Barbell in front rack.  
2. Slight dip with knees, then explode upward.  
3. Lock elbows overhead, core tight.  

## Common Mistakes
- Pressing with arms only  
- Forward lean  
- Weak leg drive  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Slow tempo  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier weight  
- Pause at bottom  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 3–6 reps  
- Olympic lifting and strength training  

## Benefits
- Explosive shoulder and trap strength  
- Leg drive integration  
- Core stability
