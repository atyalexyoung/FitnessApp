## Form Cues
1. Start bar just above knees.  
2. Pull explosively, shrug, and catch in front rack.  
3. Stand tall and stabilize.  

## Common Mistakes
- Early arm bend  
- Pulling from floor position  
- Weak hip extension  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Focus on technique  

**Harder:**  
- Add pause above knees  
- Heavier weight  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 3–5 reps  
- Power and Olympic training  

## Benefits
- Hip extension power  
- Trap and upper back development  
- Explosive strength
