## Form Cues
1. Feet hip-width, bar on floor.  
2. Pull explosively, shrug, and catch in front rack position.  
3. Stand tall, stabilize bar.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Early arm pull  
- Dropping elbows  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Power clean  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Hang clean  
- Deficit clean  

## Programming Options
- 4–6 sets × 2–4 reps  
- Power and Olympic lifting training  

## Benefits
- Explosive lower body strength  
- Upper back and trap development  
- Core stability
