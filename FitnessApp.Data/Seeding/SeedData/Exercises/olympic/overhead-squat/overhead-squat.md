## Form Cues
1. Barbell overhead with locked elbows, wide grip.  
2. Feet shoulder-width.  
3. Squat as deep as mobility allows while keeping bar stable overhead.  

## Common Mistakes
- Bar drifting forward/back  
- Knees collapsing  
- Forward torso lean  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- PVC or empty bar  
- Partial squat  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier barbell  
- Full squat depth  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 3–6 reps  
- Olympic lifting or mobility/strength combo  

## Benefits
- Shoulder and thoracic mobility  
- Core stability  
- Leg strength
