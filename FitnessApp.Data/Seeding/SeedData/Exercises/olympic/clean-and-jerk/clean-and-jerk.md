## Form Cues
1. Pull bar from floor to front rack position.  
2. Dip and drive bar overhead using legs and shoulders.  
3. Catch in split stance or push jerk.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Poor foot placement  
- Early arm bend  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Split into clean and jerk separately  
- Use lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Use heavier weight  
- Tempo variations  

## Programming Options
- 3–6 sets × 1–3 reps  
- Olympic lifting programs  

## Benefits
- Explosive power  
- Full-body strength  
- Coordination and stability
