## Form Cues
1. Barbell in front rack.  
2. Dip slightly, explode upward.  
3. Split feet, lock elbows, stabilize overhead.  

## Common Mistakes
- Forward lean  
- Feet too close together  
- Early arm bend  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Light weight  
- Focus on foot placement  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier load  
- Pause dip  

## Programming Options
- 3–6 sets × 1–3 reps  
- Olympic lifting programs  

## Benefits
- Explosive leg and shoulder strength  
- Full-body coordination  
- Stabilizes heavy loads overhead
