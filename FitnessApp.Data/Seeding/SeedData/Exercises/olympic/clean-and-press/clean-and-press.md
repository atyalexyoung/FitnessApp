## Form Cues
1. Barbell on floor, feet hip-width.  
2. Pull bar explosively, shrug and catch in front rack position.  
3. Press overhead, locking elbows and keeping core tight.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back during pull  
- Early arm bend  
- Pressing with back instead of shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use lighter weight  
- Perform clean and press separately  

**Harder:**  
- Increase weight  
- Use deficit or pause cleans  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 3–5 reps  
- Include in Olympic lifting or full-body power training  

## Benefits
- Full-body power  
- Explosive strength  
- Core and shoulder stability
