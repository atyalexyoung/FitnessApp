## Form Cues
1. Start bar above knees.  
2. Pull explosively, shrug, and drive elbows high.  
3. Keep bar close to body.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arm-dominant pull  
- Bar away from body  
- Rounded back  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Focus on shrug  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier weight  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 3–5 reps  
- Include in trap and explosive power work  

## Benefits
- Trap development  
- Explosive shoulder strength  
- Upper back power
