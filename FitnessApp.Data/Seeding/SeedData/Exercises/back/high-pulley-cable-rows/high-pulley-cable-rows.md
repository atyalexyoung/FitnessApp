## Form Cues
1. Set cable above head, use rope or bar attachment.
2. Pull toward upper chest, elbows wide.
3. Squeeze shoulder blades together.
4. Slowly release to full extension.

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Rounding back  
- Short range of motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Seated high row machine  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  
- Use for upper-back thickness  

## Benefits
- Builds traps and rear delts  
- Improves posture and scapular control  

## Tips for Success
- Focus on scapular squeeze  
- Keep chest upright  
- Avoid jerky movements
