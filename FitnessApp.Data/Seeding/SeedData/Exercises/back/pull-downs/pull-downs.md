## Form Cues
1. Grip bar slightly wider than shoulders, palms forward.
2. Sit with thighs under pad, back straight.
3. Pull bar to upper chest, elbows down and back.
4. Slowly return to full extension.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning too far back  
- Pulling behind the neck (shoulder risk)  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Assisted machine  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at bottom  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Include in vertical pull day  

## Benefits
- Builds lat width  
- Safer alternative to pull-ups for beginners  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbows  
- Keep torso upright  
- Controlled full range
