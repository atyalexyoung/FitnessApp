## Form Cues
1. Position on hyperextension bench, hips over pad.
2. Cross arms over chest or hands behind head.
3. Lower torso toward floor while keeping spine neutral.
4. Raise torso until body is in line with legs.
5. Control each repetition.

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Using momentum  
- Overextending past neutral  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Hold weight plate  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  
- Posterior chain accessory  

## Benefits
- Strengthens lower back safely  
- Builds glutes and hamstrings  
- Improves spinal stability  

## Tips for Success
- Maintain neutral spine  
- Engage glutes and hamstrings  
- Avoid swinging
