## Form Cues
1. Place one knee and hand on flat bench, other foot on floor.
2. Hold dumbbell in free hand, arm extended.
3. Pull dumbbell toward hip, keeping elbow tight.
4. Lower slowly, maintain neutral spine.
5. Repeat on other side.

## Common Mistakes
- **Rotating Torso:** Keep torso square.  
- **Jerking Weight:** Avoid momentum.  
- **Shrugging Shoulder:** Lead with elbow.  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Supported machine row  
- Lighter dumbbell  

**Harder:**  
- Heavy dumbbells  
- Slow eccentric or pause  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Accessory to pull day  

## Benefits
- Builds mid-back thickness  
- Improves balance and core stability  
- Isolates each side individually  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbow  
- Keep core engaged  
- Focus on controlled movement
