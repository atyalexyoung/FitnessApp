## Form Cues
1. Lie chest-down on incline bench or use machine pad.
2. Grab handles or bar, arms extended.
3. Pull toward torso, elbows back.
4. Slowly release, keeping chest on support.

## Common Mistakes
- Shrugging shoulders  
- Jerky motion  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Neutral grip  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  
- Use for mid-back hypertrophy  

## Benefits
- Builds back thickness safely  
- Reduces lower back load  
- Controlled motion  

## Tips for Success
- Focus on elbow drive  
- Keep chest supported  
- Smooth, controlled reps
