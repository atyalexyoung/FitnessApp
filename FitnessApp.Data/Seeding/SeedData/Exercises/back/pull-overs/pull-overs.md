## Form Cues
1. Lie flat on bench, hold dumbbell over chest.
2. Lower behind head in controlled arc.
3. Pull dumbbell back over chest using lats.
4. Keep elbows slightly bent.

## Common Mistakes
- Overstretching shoulders  
- Using momentum  
- Bending elbows excessively  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Light weight dumbbell  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at bottom  

## Programming Options
- 3 sets × 10–12 reps  
- Add as finisher for lats/chest  

## Benefits
- Expands ribcage mobility  
- Strengthens lats and chest  
- Improves posture  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Focus on lats pulling  
- Controlled, smooth motion
