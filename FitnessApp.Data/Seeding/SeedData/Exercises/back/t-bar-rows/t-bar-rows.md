## Form Cues
1. Straddle T-bar or use landmine setup.
2. Hinge slightly at hips, back neutral.
3. Grip handle, pull bar to lower chest.
4. Lower under control, avoid rounding back.

## Common Mistakes
- Back rounding  
- Using momentum  
- Elbows flaring out  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Chest supported T-bar row  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Accessory for mid-back development  

## Benefits
- Builds mid-back thickness  
- Improves lats and traps activation  
- Strengthens posterior chain  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbows  
- Maintain neutral spine  
- Focus on controlled motion
