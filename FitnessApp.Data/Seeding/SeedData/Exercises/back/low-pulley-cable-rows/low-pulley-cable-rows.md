## Form Cues
1. Sit with feet on platform, knees slightly bent.
2. Grab handle with neutral grip.
3. Pull handle to midsection, elbows back.
4. Slowly return to start, maintain posture.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning excessively  
- Using momentum  
- Shrugging shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Seated row machine  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Include for mid-back hypertrophy  

## Benefits
- Improves back thickness  
- Safer for lower back than heavy barbell rows  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbows  
- Maintain upright posture  
- Focus on controlled motion
