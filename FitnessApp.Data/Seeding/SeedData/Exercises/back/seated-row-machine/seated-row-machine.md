## Form Cues
1. Sit upright, feet on platform, grab handles.
2. Pull toward torso, elbows back.
3. Squeeze shoulder blades.
4. Return under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning too far back  
- Jerky movement  
- Shrugging shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Short range  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Mid-back mass builder  

## Benefits
- Builds mid-back thickness  
- Safer for lower back than free weights  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbows  
- Keep torso upright  
- Smooth controlled motion
