## Form Cues
1. Sit with chest against pad, grab handles.
2. Pull toward torso, elbows back.
3. Squeeze shoulder blades at peak.
4. Slowly return to start.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Shrugging shoulders  
- Jerky reps  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at contraction  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  
- Upper back hypertrophy  

## Benefits
- Builds upper and mid-back safely  
- Reduces lower back stress  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbows  
- Maintain upright chest  
- Smooth controlled motion
