## Form Cues
1. Grip bar shoulder-width, palms facing you.
2. Hang fully with shoulders engaged.
3. Pull chest toward bar using elbows.
4. Lower under control to full hang.

## Common Mistakes
- **Swinging:** Reduces lat engagement.  
- **Shrugging:** Keep shoulders down.  
- **Partial Range:** Don’t stop short of chin above bar.  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Assisted chin-ups  
- Negative reps  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted chin-ups  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × max reps  
- Include on pull day with rows  

## Benefits
- Builds biceps and lat width  
- Improves vertical pulling strength  
- Functional upper body development  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbows  
- Keep core tight  
- Avoid swinging or kipping until advanced
