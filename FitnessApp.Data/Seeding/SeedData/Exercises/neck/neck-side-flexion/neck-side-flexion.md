## Form Cues
1. Sit or stand tall.  
2. Tilt head to one side, bringing ear toward shoulder.  
3. Hold briefly and return to neutral.  

## Common Mistakes
- Shrugging shoulder  
- Rotating torso  
- Jerky motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter tilt  
- Support head lightly with hand  

**Harder:**  
- Add light resistance  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 reps per side  
- Include in warm-up, rehab, or neck mobility routine  

## Benefits
- Strengthens lateral neck muscles  
- Improves cervical mobility  
- Reduces tension  

## Tips for Success
- Keep shoulders relaxed  
- Move slowly  
- Avoid pain
