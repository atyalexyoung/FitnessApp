## Form Cues
1. Sit or stand tall.  
2. Slowly lower chin toward chest.  
3. Hold briefly and return to neutral.  

## Common Mistakes
- Jerky motion  
- Rounding shoulders  
- Over-flexing  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter range of motion  
- Support head with hands  

**Harder:**  
- Add resistance with hands or band  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Can include in warm-up, rehab, or neck strength routine  

## Benefits
- Strengthens anterior neck muscles  
- Improves cervical mobility  
- Supports posture  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly  
- Keep shoulders relaxed  
- Avoid pain
