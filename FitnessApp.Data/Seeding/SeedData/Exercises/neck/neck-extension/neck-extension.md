## Form Cues
1. Sit or stand tall.  
2. Slowly tilt head backward, looking up.  
3. Hold briefly and return to neutral.  

## Common Mistakes
- Overextending  
- Shrugging shoulders  
- Jerky motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter range of motion  
- Support head lightly with hands  

**Harder:**  
- Add light resistance  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in warm-up, rehab, or neck mobility routine  

## Benefits
- Strengthens posterior neck muscles  
- Improves cervical extension mobility  
- Supports posture and upper-back health  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly  
- Keep shoulders relaxed  
- Avoid pain
