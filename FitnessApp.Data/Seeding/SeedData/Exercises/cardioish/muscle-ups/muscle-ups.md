## Form Cues
1. Grip bar, pull explosively to chest.  
2. Transition into dip over bar.  
3. Press up to full lockout, then lower under control.  

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum poorly  
- Incomplete lockout  
- Shoulder strain  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Band-assisted  
- Jumping muscle-ups  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted muscle-ups  
- Strict form only  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 3–8 reps  
- Include in HIIT or advanced circuits  

## Benefits
- Upper body explosive strength  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Core and grip engagement  

## Tips for Success
- Explosive pull, controlled transition  
- Engage core  
- Warm-up shoulders and elbows
