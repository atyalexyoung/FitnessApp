## Form Cues
1. Stand upright, feet on steps.  
2. Step continuously, maintain steady rhythm.  
3. Avoid leaning on handles excessively.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Short steps  
- Uneven pace  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower speed  

**Harder:**  
- Higher speed  
- Weighted vest  

## Programming Options
- 15–30 min steady-state  
- Interval: 1–2 min fast / 1–2 min slow  

## Benefits
- Lower body endurance  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Glute and quad strength  

## Tips for Success
- Upright posture  
- Smooth stepping  
- Engage core slightly
