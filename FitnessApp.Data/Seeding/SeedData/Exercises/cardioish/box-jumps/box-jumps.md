## Form Cues
1. Stand in front of box, feet shoulder-width.  
2. Swing arms, explode up, landing softly on the box.  
3. Step or jump back down, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Knees caving  
- Hard landings  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower box  
- Step-ups instead  

**Harder:**  
- Higher box  
- Weighted vest  
- Lateral box jumps  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Include in HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower body power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Agility and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Land softly  
- Engage glutes and quads  
- Use arm swing for momentum
