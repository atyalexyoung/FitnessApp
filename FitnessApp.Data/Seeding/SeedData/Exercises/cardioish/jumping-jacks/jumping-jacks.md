## Form Cues
1. Start standing.  
2. Jump feet out, raise arms overhead.  
3. Return to start.  

## Common Mistakes
- Landing heavily  
- Not fully raising arms  
- Poor posture  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Step jacks  
- Slow tempo  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted jacks  
- Jump higher  
- Add squat  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 30–60s  
- Include in warm-up or circuit  

## Benefits
- Cardio conditioning  
- Full-body activation  
- Coordination & rhythm  

## Tips for Success
- Land softly  
- Full range motion  
- Maintain upright torso
