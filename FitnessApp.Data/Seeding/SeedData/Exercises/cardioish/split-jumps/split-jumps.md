## Form Cues
1. Start in split stance.  
2. Jump, switching front and back leg mid-air.  
3. Land softly, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Knees caving  
- Leaning forward  
- Hard landings  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Step back instead of jumping  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Dumbbells  
- Weighted vest  
- Pause mid-air  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps per leg  
- Include in HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower body explosive power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Unilateral strength  

## Tips for Success
- Land softly  
- Keep torso upright  
- Engage glutes on jump
