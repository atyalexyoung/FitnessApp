## Form Cues
1. Hold medicine ball above one shoulder.  
2. Chop diagonally across body to opposite hip.  
3. Return to start, repeat on both sides.  

## Common Mistakes
- Twisting knees or back  
- Using arms only  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter ball  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier ball  
- Explosive pace  
- Include squat before chop  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps per side  
- Include in HIIT/core circuits  

## Benefits
- Oblique and core strength  
- Rotational power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Engage obliques  
- Controlled chop  
- Slight pivot for hips
