## Form Cues
1. Hold sledgehammer with both hands.  
2. Swing overhead, striking tire with controlled motion.  
3. Return to start, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Twisting back excessively  
- Poor grip  
- Hitting with arms only  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter hammer  
- Shorter swings  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier hammer  
- Longer sets  
- Alternating swing directions  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 15–25 swings  
- Include in HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Full-body conditioning  
- Cardiovascular endurance  
- Grip and core strength  

## Tips for Success
- Engage core and legs  
- Controlled swings  
- Keep neutral spine
