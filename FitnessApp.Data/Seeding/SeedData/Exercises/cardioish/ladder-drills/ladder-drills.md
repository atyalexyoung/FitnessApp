## Form Cues
1. Set ladder on floor.  
2. Perform rapid footwork patterns (two-feet, in/out, lateral).  
3. Keep light on feet, maintain rhythm.  

## Common Mistakes
- Heavy steps  
- Incorrect ladder pattern  
- Poor posture  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slower pace  
- Simplify pattern  

**Harder:**  
- Faster pace  
- Complex foot patterns  
- Add push-ups or burpees between rounds  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 rounds × 30–60s  
- Include in HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Agility  
- Coordination  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Stay light on feet  
- Focus on precision  
- Maintain posture
