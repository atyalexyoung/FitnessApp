## Form Cues
1. Stand feet shoulder-width, kettlebell in hands.  
2. Hinge at hips, swing kettlebell between legs.  
3. Thrust hips forward, swing kettlebell to chest height.  
4. Control descent and repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Using arms instead of hips  
- Rounding back  
- Overextending shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Shorter sets  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier kettlebell  
- Longer sets  
- Single-arm swing  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 12–20 reps  
- Include in HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Posterior chain power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Hip hinge mechanics  

## Tips for Success
- Engage glutes and core  
- Maintain neutral spine  
- Thrust with hips, not arms
