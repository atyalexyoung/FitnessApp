## Form Cues
1. Adjust seat and handlebars.  
2. Pedal smoothly, keep cadence steady.  
3. Maintain upright torso or slight forward lean.  

## Common Mistakes
- Incorrect seat height  
- Sloppy pedal stroke  
- Hunching  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower resistance  
- Shorter ride  

**Harder:**  
- Higher resistance  
- Standing sprints  
- Intervals  

## Programming Options
- 20–60 min steady ride  
- HIIT: 30s sprint / 30s easy × 8–12  

## Benefits
- Cardiovascular endurance  
- Low-impact  
- Leg strength  

## Tips for Success
- Smooth pedal motion  
- Engage core  
- Proper seat height
