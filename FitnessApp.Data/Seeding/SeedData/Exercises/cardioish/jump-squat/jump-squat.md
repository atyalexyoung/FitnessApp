## Form Cues
1. Stand shoulder-width, toes slightly out.  
2. Descend into squat.  
3. Explosively jump as high as possible.  
4. Land softly, descend into next rep.  

## Common Mistakes
- Knees caving  
- Not landing softly  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight partial squat jump  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted jump squat  
- Slow eccentric descent  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in HIIT or circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower body power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Explosive strength  

## Tips for Success
- Soft landing  
- Knees tracking toes  
- Engage glutes on jump
