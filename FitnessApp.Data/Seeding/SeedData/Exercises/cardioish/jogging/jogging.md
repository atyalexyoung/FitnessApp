## Form Cues
1. Stand tall, relax shoulders.  
2. Swing arms naturally with stride.  
3. Maintain moderate pace, breathing steady.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Overstriding  
- Heel striking heavily  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slow pace  
- Walk-jog intervals  

**Harder:**  
- Faster pace  
- Hills or inclines  
- Fartlek intervals
