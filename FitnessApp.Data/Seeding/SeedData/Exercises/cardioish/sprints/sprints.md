## Form Cues
1. Start in athletic stance or standing.  
2. Sprint as fast as possible for short distance.  
3. Walk or jog to recover, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning back  
- Overstriding  
- Poor arm drive  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter distance  
- Submaximal effort  

**Harder:**  
- Max effort  
- Uphill sprints  
- Weighted sled  

## Programming Options
- 8–12 × 20–60m sprints with full recovery  
- Include in HIIT sessions  

## Benefits
- Explosive leg power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Anaerobic capacity  

## Tips for Success
- Maintain upright posture  
- Drive arms  
- Controlled recovery
