## Form Cues
1. Stand shoulder-width.  
2. Swing arms, bend knees, jump forward as far as possible.  
3. Land softly, absorb impact with knees slightly bent.  

## Common Mistakes
- Knees collapsing  
- Leaning forward  
- Not absorbing landing  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter jump  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted vest  
- Multiple reps consecutively  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 6–10 reps  
- Include in HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Explosive lower body power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Leg strength  

## Tips for Success
- Land softly  
- Engage glutes and quads  
- Use arm swing for momentum
