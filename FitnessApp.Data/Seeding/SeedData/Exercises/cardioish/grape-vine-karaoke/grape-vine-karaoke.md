## Form Cues
1. Stand tall, feet hip-width.  
2. Step laterally, crossing one foot in front, then behind.  
3. Continue side-stepping rhythmically.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Stiff knees  
- Poor rhythm  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slower pace  
- Smaller steps  

**Harder:**  
- Faster pace  
- Add arm movements  
- Increase distance  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in warm-ups or circuits  

## Benefits
- Lateral agility  
- Coordination  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest upright  
- Step lightly  
- Maintain rhythm
