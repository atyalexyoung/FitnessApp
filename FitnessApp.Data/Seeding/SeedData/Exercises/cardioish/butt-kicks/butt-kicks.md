## Form Cues
1. Stand tall, feet hip-width.  
2. Kick heels toward glutes alternately, fast pace.  
3. Pump arms naturally.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Short stride  
- Poor arm drive  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slower pace  
- Short duration  

**Harder:**  
- Faster intervals  
- Longer sets  
- Add light jump  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in HIIT or warm-ups  

## Benefits
- Hamstring activation  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Speed and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest upright  
- Pump arms  
- Full heel-to-glute motion
