## Form Cues
1. Start in high plank.  
2. Drive one knee toward chest, then alternate rapidly.  
3. Keep hips low and core tight.  

## Common Mistakes
- Hips too high  
- Poor plank alignment  
- Uneven pace  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slower pace  
- Hands on elevated surface  

**Harder:**  
- Fast intervals  
- Add sliders or resistance bands  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Core activation  
- Cardiovascular endurance  
- Agility and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Maintain plank  
- Drive knees fully  
- Breathe rhythmically
