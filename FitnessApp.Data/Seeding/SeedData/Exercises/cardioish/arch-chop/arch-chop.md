## Form Cues
1. Stand feet shoulder-width, hold medicine ball overhead.  
2. Rotate torso, chopping ball diagonally to opposite hip.  
3. Return to start and repeat on both sides.  

## Common Mistakes
- Twisting knees or hips  
- Using arms instead of core  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter ball  
- Smaller range of motion  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier ball  
- Explosive movements  
- Perform as fast-paced intervals  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps per side  
- Include in core circuits or HIIT  

## Benefits
- Oblique and core strength  
- Rotational power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips stable  
- Engage obliques  
- Controlled motion
