## Form Cues
1. Set treadmill incline or find hill.  
2. Walk at steady pace, lean slightly forward at hips.  
3. Maintain long stride and steady arm swing.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning too far forward  
- Short steps  
- Holding handles  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower incline  

**Harder:**  
- Higher incline  
- Weighted vest  

## Programming Options
- 15–45 min steady incline  
- Interval: 1–2 min incline / 1–2 min flat  

## Benefits
- Glute & hamstring activation  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Low-impact leg strength  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled posture  
- Smooth steps  
- Avoid gripping treadmill handles
