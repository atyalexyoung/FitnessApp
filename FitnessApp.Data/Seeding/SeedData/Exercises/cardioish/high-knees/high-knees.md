## Form Cues
1. Stand tall, feet hip-width.  
2. Drive knees toward chest rapidly, alternating legs.  
3. Pump arms for momentum.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning back  
- Low knee drive  
- Shuffling feet  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slower pace  
- Lower knee height  

**Harder:**  
- Faster pace  
- Add resistance bands  
- Perform as sprint intervals  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in HIIT or agility circuits  

## Benefits
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Hip flexor strength  
- Speed and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Engage core  
- Pump arms  
- Maintain upright posture
