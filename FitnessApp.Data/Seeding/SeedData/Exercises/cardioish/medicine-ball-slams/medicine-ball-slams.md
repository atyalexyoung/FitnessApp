## Form Cues
1. Hold medicine ball overhead, feet shoulder-width.  
2. Slam ball to the ground explosively.  
3. Squat to pick it up, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Using arms only  
- Shallow slam  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter ball  
- Slower pace  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier ball  
- Explosive pace  
- Jump before slam  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in HIIT/core circuits  

## Benefits
- Full-body power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Core engagement  

## Tips for Success
- Engage core  
- Use legs and hips  
- Controlled explosive movement
