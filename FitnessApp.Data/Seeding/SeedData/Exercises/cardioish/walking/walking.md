## Form Cues
1. Stand tall, relax shoulders.  
2. Step naturally, maintain moderate pace.  
3. Swing arms gently to aid movement.  

## Common Mistakes
- Slouching  
- Short steps  
- Looking down  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Flat surface  

**Harder:**  
- Inclines  
- Weighted vest  
- Power walking
