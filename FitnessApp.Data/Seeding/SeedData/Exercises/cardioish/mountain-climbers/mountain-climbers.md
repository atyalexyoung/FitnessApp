## Form Cues
1. Start in plank position.  
2. Drive one knee toward chest.  
3. Alternate legs quickly while maintaining plank.  

## Common Mistakes
- Hips sagging  
- Incomplete knee drive  
- Uneven tempo  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slower pace  
- Step instead of run  

**Harder:**  
- Cross-body drive  
- Weighted vest  
- Elevated feet  

## Programming Options
- 30s work / 30s rest × 8–12 rounds  
- Include in circuit or EMOM  

## Benefits
- Core stability & endurance  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Full-body engagement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Controlled arm placement  
- Smooth breathing
