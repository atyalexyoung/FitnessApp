## Form Cues
1. Hold dumbbells or kettlebells at your sides.  
2. Stand tall, shoulders back.  
3. Walk forward with controlled steps, maintaining upright posture.  

## Common Mistakes
- Slouching  
- Short strides  
- Dropping weights  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weights  
- Shorter distance  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier weights  
- Longer distance  
- Single-arm carry  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 rounds × 20–40m  
- Include in circuits or conditioning  

## Benefits
- Grip and forearm strength  
- Core stability  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Keep shoulders back  
- Engage core  
- Step deliberately
