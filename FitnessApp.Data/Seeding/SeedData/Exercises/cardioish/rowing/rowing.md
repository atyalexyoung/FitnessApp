## Form Cues
1. Sit on rowing machine, feet strapped, grip handle.  
2. Drive with legs, then lean back slightly, pulling handle to chest.  
3. Extend arms forward, return to start, repeat smoothly.  

## Common Mistakes
- Using arms too early  
- Rounding back  
- Short strokes  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower resistance  
- Shorter sessions  

**Harder:**  
- Increase resistance  
- Longer intervals  
- Sprint intervals  

## Programming Options
- 10–20 min steady-state  
- Intervals: 30s fast / 30s easy × 8–12  
- Part of HIIT circuit  

## Benefits
- Full-body endurance  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Low-impact on joints  

## Tips for Success
- Smooth, controlled strokes  
- Engage core  
- Full leg drive
