## Form Cues
1. Start in athletic stance, knees slightly bent.  
2. Shuffle laterally, keeping chest upright and feet light.  
3. Change direction at markers or cones.  

## Common Mistakes
- Crossing feet  
- Standing upright  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter distance  
- Slower pace  

**Harder:**  
- Longer distance  
- Faster pace  
- Add push-ups or burpees between shuffles  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in agility or HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Lateral agility  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Leg strength and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips low  
- Step lightly  
- Control direction changes
