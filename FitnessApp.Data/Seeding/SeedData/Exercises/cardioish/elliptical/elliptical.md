## Form Cues
1. Stand upright, hands on handles.  
2. Push and pull handles while moving feet in elliptical motion.  
3. Maintain smooth, even stride.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Overstriding  
- Uneven stride  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower resistance  

**Harder:**  
- Higher resistance  
- Sprint intervals  
- Reverse stride  

## Programming Options
- 20–45 min steady-state  
- Interval: 1–2 min fast / 1–2 min easy  

## Benefits
- Cardiovascular endurance  
- Low-impact leg training  
- Full-body coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Smooth stride  
- Engage arms and core  
- Maintain upright posture
