## Form Cues
1. Maintain streamlined position in water.  
2. Use proper stroke technique (freestyle, breaststroke, etc.).  
3. Breathe rhythmically, maintain continuous motion.  

## Common Mistakes
- Poor breathing timing  
- Dropping hips  
- Overreaching  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Short distance swims  
- Use kickboard  

**Harder:**  
- Longer intervals  
- Faster pace  
- Include sprints  

## Programming Options
- 20–45 min steady swim  
- Interval training: 50–100m sprint / 50m recovery  

## Benefits
- Full-body conditioning  
- Cardiovascular endurance  
- Low-impact on joints  

## Tips for Success
- Smooth strokes  
- Controlled breathing  
- Engage core
