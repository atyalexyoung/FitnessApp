## Form Cues
1. Start in athletic stance.  
2. Push off one leg into long stride or hop.  
3. Land softly, immediately bound off opposite leg.  

## Common Mistakes
- Poor knee tracking  
- Hard landings  
- Not engaging hips  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter bounds  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted vest  
- Increase distance  
- Include arm drive  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 6–12 bounds per leg  
- Include in sprint or plyometric circuits  

## Benefits
- Explosive lower body power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Agility and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Land softly  
- Engage glutes and quads  
- Use arms for momentum
