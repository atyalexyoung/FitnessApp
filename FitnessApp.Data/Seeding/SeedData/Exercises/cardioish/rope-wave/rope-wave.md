## Form Cues
1. Hold ropes in both hands.  
2. Generate waves using alternating or simultaneous arm motions.  
3. Maintain slight squat, core engaged.  

## Common Mistakes
- Straight arms without momentum  
- Rounding back  
- Standing upright  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter ropes  
- Slower pace  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier ropes  
- Longer duration  
- Add squats or jumps  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Upper body and core strength  
- Grip endurance  

## Tips for Success
- Slight squat  
- Engage core  
- Smooth, controlled waves
