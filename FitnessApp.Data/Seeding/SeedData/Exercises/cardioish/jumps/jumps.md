## Form Cues
1. Stand feet shoulder-width.  
2. Bend knees and swing arms.  
3. Explosively jump upward, land softly.  
4. Reset and repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Knees caving  
- Hard landings  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller jump  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted vest  
- Tuck jump  
- Higher jump  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in HIIT or plyometric circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower body explosive power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Agility  

## Tips for Success
- Land softly  
- Engage glutes and quads  
- Use arms for momentum
