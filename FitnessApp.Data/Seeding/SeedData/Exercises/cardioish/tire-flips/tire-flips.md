## Form Cues
1. Stand close to tire, feet shoulder-width.  
2. Squat and grip underside.  
3. Drive through legs and hips, flipping tire forward.  
4. Reset and repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Using back instead of legs  
- Rounding spine  
- Poor grip  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter tire  
- Partial flips  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier tire  
- Consecutive flips  
- Add sprint after flip  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 6–10 flips  
- Include in HIIT or strongman circuits  

## Benefits
- Explosive power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  
- Full-body strength  

## Tips for Success
- Engage legs and hips  
- Keep neutral spine  
- Use controlled movements
