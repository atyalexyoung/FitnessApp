## Form Cues
1. Stand feet shoulder-width, kettlebell in one hand.  
2. Hinge at hips, swing kettlebell between legs.  
3. Thrust hips forward, swing kettlebell to chest height.  
4. Control descent, switch arms if doing sets.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rotating torso excessively  
- Rounding back  
- Using arms to lift  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Fewer reps  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier weight  
- Longer sets  
- Alternating swings  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 12–15 reps per arm  
- Include in circuits or HIIT  

## Benefits
- Posterior chain power  
- Core stability  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Engage glutes and core  
- Keep neutral spine  
- Thrust with hips
