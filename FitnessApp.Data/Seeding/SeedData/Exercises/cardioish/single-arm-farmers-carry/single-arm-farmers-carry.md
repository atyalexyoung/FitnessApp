## Form Cues
1. Hold weight in one hand at your side.  
2. Stand tall, engage core.  
3. Walk steadily, keeping balance.  
4. Switch hands and repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning to one side  
- Slouching  
- Rushing steps  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Short distance  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier weight  
- Longer distance  
- Walk backward  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 rounds × 20–40m each arm  
- Include in circuits  

## Benefits
- Unilateral core stability  
- Grip and forearm strength  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Keep torso upright  
- Control steps  
- Engage obliques
