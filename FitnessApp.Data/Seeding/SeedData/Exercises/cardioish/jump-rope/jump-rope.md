## Form Cues
1. Hold rope handles, elbows close.  
2. Swing rope overhead and jump lightly over rope.  
3. Land on balls of feet.  

## Common Mistakes
- Jumping too high  
- Poor wrist rotation  
- Landing flat-footed  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slower pace  
- Double under skipped  

**Harder:**  
- High knees jump rope  
- Double unders  
- Crossovers  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 rounds × 1–3 min  
- Interval: 30s on / 30s off  
- Endurance: continuous 10–20 min  

## Benefits
- Cardio conditioning  
- Coordination & timing  
- Calf & lower leg endurance  

## Tips for Success
- Soft landing  
- Wrists rotate rope, not arms  
- Maintain upright posture
