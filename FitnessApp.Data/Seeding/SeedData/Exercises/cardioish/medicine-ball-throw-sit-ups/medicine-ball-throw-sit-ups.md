## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, knees bent, hold medicine ball at chest.  
2. Perform sit-up, tossing ball forward or to partner.  
3. Catch ball, return to start, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Jerking head or neck  
- Using momentum instead of core  
- Landing ball improperly  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter ball  
- Slower pace  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier ball  
- Partner toss or wall throw  
- Explosive pace  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in HIIT or core circuits  

## Benefits
- Core strength and stability  
- Explosive power  
- Cardiovascular conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Engage abs  
- Avoid neck strain  
- Controlled toss and catch
