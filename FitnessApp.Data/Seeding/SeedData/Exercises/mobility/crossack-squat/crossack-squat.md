## Form Cues
1. Stand wide with toes pointing forward.  
2. Shift weight to one leg, bending knee while keeping other leg straight.  
3. Keep chest up and back straight, lowering hips.  
4. Push through heel to return to center and repeat on other side.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Heels lifting off ground  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Hold onto support  
- Shallow squat  

**Harder:**  
- Add dumbbell or kettlebell  
- Deeper squat  
- Slow tempo  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 6–12 reps per side  
- Include in warm-up or mobility circuits  

## Benefits
- Hip and ankle mobility  
- Adductor flexibility  
- Lower body stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest tall  
- Move controlled  
- Focus on ankle dorsiflexion
