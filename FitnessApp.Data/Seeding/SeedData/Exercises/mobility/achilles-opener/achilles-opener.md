## Form Cues
1. Place forefoot against wall or surface.  
2. Push knee forward gently, keeping heel down.  
3. Hold stretch and maintain upright posture.  

## Common Mistakes
- Heel lifting  
- Leaning forward  
- Bouncing  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller lean  
- Shorter hold  

**Harder:**  
- Longer hold  
- Add ankle circles  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in warm-up or mobility routine  

## Benefits
- Improves ankle dorsiflexion  
- Enhances calf flexibility  
- Supports squats and lunges  

## Tips for Success
- Keep heel down  
- Move slow and controlled  
- Engage core
