## Form Cues
1. Stand facing wall, hands on wall for support.  
2. Step one leg back, keeping heel on the floor.  
3. Lean forward slightly, feeling stretch in calf.  

## Common Mistakes
- Heel lifting  
- Locking knee of front leg  
- Leaning torso too far  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slightly bend back knee  
- Step closer to wall  

**Harder:**  
- Keep leg straight and hold longer  
- Press deeper into stretch  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per leg  
- Include in warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Calf flexibility  
- Ankle mobility  
- Reduces tightness  

## Tips for Success
- Keep back heel on ground  
- Move slowly  
- Breathe deeply
