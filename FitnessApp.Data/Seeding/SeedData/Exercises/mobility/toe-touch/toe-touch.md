## Form Cues
1. Stand or sit with legs extended.  
2. Hinge at hips to reach toward toes.  
3. Keep knees slightly bent if needed.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding spine excessively  
- Locking knees  
- Jerky movement  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees slightly  
- Reach to shins  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Reach further with straight legs  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–30s  
- Include in warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Hamstring flexibility  
- Lower back mobility  
- Calf stretch  

## Tips for Success
- Hinge at hips  
- Keep spine long  
- Move slowly
