## Form Cues
1. Hold a weight (dumbbell or plate) at forehead.  
2. Circle it slowly around head in controlled motion.  
3. Keep elbows slightly bent and core engaged.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rapid movement  
- Leaning torso  
- Locking elbows  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use lighter weight  
- Shorter arc  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier weight  
- Full slow circle  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 6–10 reps each direction  
- Include in warm-up or shoulder mobility routines  

## Benefits
- Improves shoulder rotation  
- Thoracic spine mobility  
- Core stability  

## Tips for Success
- Move controlled  
- Maintain posture  
- Focus on quality of motion
