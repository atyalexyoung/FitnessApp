## Form Cues
1. Start in side-lying position, bottom leg bent, top leg straight.  
2. Open top leg to ceiling, then lower it slowly.  
3. Keep hips stacked and core engaged.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rolling hips  
- Swinging leg  
- Bending torso  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller range of motion  
- Support head with hand  

**Harder:**  
- Add ankle weights  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 reps per leg  
- Include in mobility or warm-up  

## Benefits
- Hip mobility  
- Glute activation  
- Hip stabilizer strengthening  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly  
- Engage core  
- Keep hips stacked
