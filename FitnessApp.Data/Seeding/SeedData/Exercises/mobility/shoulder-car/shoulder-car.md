## Form Cues
1. Raise arm and rotate through full range of motion slowly.  
2. Keep torso stable and core engaged.  
3. Perform in both directions for balance.  

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Leaning torso  
- Partial rotation  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller ROM  
- Perform seated  

**Harder:**  
- Full ROM  
- Add light band resistance  

## Programming Options
- 2–4 sets × 5–10 reps per side  
- Include pre-workout or rehab  

## Benefits
- Shoulder mobility and stability  
- Improves posture  
- Reduces injury risk  

## Tips for Success
- Move slow and controlled  
- Engage core  
- Focus on joint quality
