## Form Cues
1. Stand tall, arms extended to sides.  
2. Make small circles forward, gradually increasing size.  
3. Reverse direction after 10–20 reps.  

## Common Mistakes
- Hunching shoulders  
- Too fast  
- Not controlled  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller circles  
- Support arms with wall  

**Harder:**  
- Larger circles  
- Hold light weights  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–20 reps each direction  
- Include in warm-up  

## Benefits
- Shoulder mobility  
- Increases blood flow  
- Prepares joints for lifting  

## Tips for Success
- Keep torso stable  
- Move slowly and controlled  
- Maintain shoulder engagement
