## Form Cues
1. Raise one arm overhead, bend elbow, reach down back.  
2. Use opposite hand to gently pull elbow.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning torso  
- Neck tension  
- Pulling too hard  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter reach  
- Support with wall  

**Harder:**  
- Pull deeper  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–30s per arm  
- Include in warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Shoulder mobility  
- Lat stretch  
- Thoracic extension  

## Tips for Success
- Keep spine neutral  
- Move gently  
- Relax neck
