## Form Cues
1. Stand with wide stance, arms extended.  
2. Rotate torso, reaching one arm toward opposite foot.  
3. Keep back straight, engage core.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding spine  
- Locking knees  
- Moving too fast  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter reach  
- Bend knees slightly  

**Harder:**  
- Hold at end range  
- Deeper torso rotation  
- Add pulses  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 6–10 reps per side  
- Include in mobility or warm-up flow  

## Benefits
- Spinal and shoulder mobility  
- Hip flexibility  
- Core activation  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest open  
- Move slowly  
- Engage core
