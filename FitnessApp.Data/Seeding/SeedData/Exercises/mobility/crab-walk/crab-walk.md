## Form Cues
1. Sit with feet and hands on floor, hips lifted.  
2. Move forward/backward using hands and feet.  
3. Keep core engaged and hips lifted.  

## Common Mistakes
- Hips sagging  
- Shoulders collapsing  
- Fast, uncontrolled movement  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller steps  
- Pause frequently  

**Harder:**  
- Longer distance  
- Slow tempo  
- Add side steps  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 steps  
- Include in warm-up or mobility flow  

## Benefits
- Posterior chain activation  
- Shoulder and hip mobility  
- Core stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips lifted  
- Engage core  
- Move slow and controlled
