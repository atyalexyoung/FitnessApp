## Form Cues
1. Bring one arm across chest.  
2. Hold with opposite arm, keeping shoulder down.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Lifting shoulder  
- Torso rotation  
- Jerky motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend arm less  
- Hold shorter  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Lean slightly for deeper stretch  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–30s per arm  
- Include in warm-up or post-workout stretch  

## Benefits
- Posterior shoulder flexibility  
- Trapezius and rhomboid stretch  
- Prepares for pressing movements  

## Tips for Success
- Keep shoulders down  
- Relax neck  
- Move gently
