## Form Cues
1. Step one foot forward, back foot perpendicular.  
2. Bend front knee, extend arms sideways at shoulder height.  
3. Keep chest tall and gaze forward.  

## Common Mistakes
- Front knee caving  
- Leaning torso  
- Rounded shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter stance  
- Support back heel  

**Harder:**  
- Longer hold  
- Deep lunge  
- Add arm pulses  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s hold per side  
- Include in warm-up, mobility, or yoga flow  

## Benefits
- Hip, groin, and leg mobility  
- Shoulder and thoracic stability  
- Improves balance  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest tall  
- Align front knee over ankle  
- Move controlled
