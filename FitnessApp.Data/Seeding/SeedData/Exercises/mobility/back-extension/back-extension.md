## Form Cues
1. Lie prone or on extension bench.  
2. Lift chest slightly, keeping glutes engaged.  
3. Hold, then slowly lower.  

## Common Mistakes
- Overarching lower back  
- Jerky motion  
- Neck strain  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Hands under shoulders for support  
- Smaller lift  

**Harder:**  
- Hold at top longer  
- Add arm extension  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in mobility or posterior chain warm-up  

## Benefits
- Lower back mobility  
- Glute and hamstring stretch  

## Tips for Success
- Move slow  
- Engage glutes and core  
- Avoid neck strain
