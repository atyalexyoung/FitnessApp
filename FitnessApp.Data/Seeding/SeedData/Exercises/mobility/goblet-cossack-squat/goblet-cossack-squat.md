## Form Cues
1. Hold dumbbell/kettlebell close to chest in goblet position.  
2. Perform lateral Cossack squat keeping chest tall and core tight.  
3. Return to center and repeat on other side.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Heels lifting  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Shallow squat  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier goblet weight  
- Deeper squat  
- Slow tempo  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 6–12 reps per side  
- Include in warm-up, mobility, or leg circuits  

## Benefits
- Hip and ankle mobility  
- Lower body stability  
- Core activation  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest tall  
- Move controlled  
- Focus on ankle dorsiflexion
