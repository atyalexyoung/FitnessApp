## Form Cues
1. Stand or sit tall, arms overhead.  
2. Lean gently to one side, keeping hips squared.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Bending forward  
- Rotating hips  
- Jerky motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter reach  
- Support arm on wall  

**Harder:**  
- Extend arms further  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Lateral spine stretch  
- Oblique activation  
- Improves posture  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips square  
- Engage core  
- Move slow
