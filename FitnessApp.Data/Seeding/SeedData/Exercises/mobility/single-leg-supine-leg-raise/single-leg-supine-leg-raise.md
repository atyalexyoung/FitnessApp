## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, extend one leg up toward ceiling.  
2. Keep other leg flat on floor.  
3. Hold stretch and gently pulse if needed.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding lower back  
- Locking knee  
- Overstretching hamstrings  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Slight bend in raised leg  
- Use strap to assist  

**Harder:**  
- Straight leg with flexed foot  
- Pulse gently at top  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–4 sets × 20–40s per leg  
- Include in warm-ups or cool-downs  

## Benefits
- Hamstring and hip mobility  
- Reduces lower back tension  
- Improves leg flexibility  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core engaged  
- Avoid overstretching  
- Breathe steadily
