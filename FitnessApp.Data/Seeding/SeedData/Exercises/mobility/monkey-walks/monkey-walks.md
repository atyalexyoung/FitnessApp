## Form Cues
1. Start in deep squat.  
2. Step forward, maintaining squat, chest tall.  
3. Move hands forward for balance, hips low.  

## Common Mistakes
- Torso leaning forward  
- Heels lifting  
- Rushing steps  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shallow squat  
- Use wall for support  

**Harder:**  
- Deeper squat  
- Slow tempo  
- Add rotation or arm reach  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 steps  
- Include in warm-up or mobility flows  

## Benefits
- Hip and hamstring mobility  
- Glute activation  
- Core stability and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest tall  
- Heels down  
- Move controlled
