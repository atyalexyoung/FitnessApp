## Form Cues
1. Extend limb slowly while keeping other joints stable.  
2. Move joint to gently tension nerve path.  
3. Hold briefly and return to start.  

## Common Mistakes
- Jerking movement  
- Overstretching  
- Leaning torso  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller range of motion  
- Shorter hold  

**Harder:**  
- Full range  
- Longer hold  
- Add repetitions  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 6–10 reps per limb  
- Include in warm-up or recovery mobility  

## Benefits
- Improves neural mobility  
- Enhances flexibility  
- Reduces joint stiffness  

## Tips for Success
- Move slow and controlled  
- Focus on gentle tension  
- Maintain core engagement
