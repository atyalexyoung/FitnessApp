## Form Cues
1. Stand on one leg, hinge at hips, extend other leg backward.  
2. Arms extend forward or sideways for balance.  
3. Keep torso parallel to floor, core engaged.  

## Common Mistakes
- Torso collapsing  
- Hip dropping  
- Locked standing knee  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Hands on hips  
- Slight forward lean  

**Harder:**  
- Reach arms fully forward  
- Hold longer  
- Add small pulses  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–30s per side  
- Include in balance, mobility, or yoga flow  

## Benefits
- Hamstring, glute, and hip strength  
- Core stabilization  
- Balance improvement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips level  
- Engage glutes and core  
- Move slow and controlled
