## Form Cues
1. Lie supine, knees bent, feet flat.  
2. Press hips up, shoulders on floor, chest open.  
3. Hold, then lower with control.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back excessively  
- Shoulders lifting off floor  
- Rapid motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower lift  
- Hold shorter  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Add leg extension  
- Add pulses  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–20 reps  
- Include in posterior chain or mobility routines  

## Benefits
- Glute and lower back activation  
- Shoulder and thoracic mobility  
- Core engagement  

## Tips for Success
- Engage glutes  
- Keep shoulders relaxed  
- Move controlled
