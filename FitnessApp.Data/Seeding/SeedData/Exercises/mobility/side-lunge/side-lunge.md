## Form Cues
1. Step wide, bend one knee while keeping the other leg straight.  
2. Keep torso upright, hips back.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Heels lifting  
- Knee collapsing inward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shallower lunge  
- Support with wall  

**Harder:**  
- Deeper stretch  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in warm-up or mobility  

## Benefits
- Hip adductor mobility  
- Glute stretch  
- Core engagement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep back straight  
- Hips back  
- Move controlled
