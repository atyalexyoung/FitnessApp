## Form Cues
1. Step forward into lunge, back knee slightly bent.  
2. Keep torso upright, hips square.  
3. Hold and breathe deeply.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Knee over toes of front foot  
- Hips twisting  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter lunge  
- Hold support  

**Harder:**  
- Deeper lunge  
- Add arm reach overhead  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in warm-up or mobility flow  

## Benefits
- Hip flexor stretch  
- Quad activation  
- Glute engagement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest tall  
- Engage glutes and core  
- Avoid knee strain
