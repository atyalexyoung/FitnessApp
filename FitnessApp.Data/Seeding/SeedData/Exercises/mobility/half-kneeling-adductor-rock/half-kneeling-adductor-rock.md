## Form Cues
1. Start in half-kneeling position.  
2. Rock hips forward and back, feeling stretch in adductors.  
3. Maintain upright torso and core engagement.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning torso forward  
- Collapsing hips  
- Moving too fast  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter range of motion  
- Use hands for balance  

**Harder:**  
- Deeper hip rock  
- Slow tempo  
- Hold at end range  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 8–12 reps per side  
- Include in hip mobility or yoga flows  

## Benefits
- Adductor and hip flexor mobility  
- Core stability  
- Improves lunge mechanics  

## Tips for Success
- Move controlled  
- Keep chest tall  
- Engage glutes and core
