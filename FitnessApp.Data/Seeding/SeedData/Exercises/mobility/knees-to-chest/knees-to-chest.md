## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, bring knees to chest.  
2. Hold gently, keeping shoulders relaxed.  
3. Rock side to side if desired.  

## Common Mistakes
- Neck tension  
- Pulling knees too forcefully  
- Lifting shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Pull one knee at a time  
- Use strap if needed  

**Harder:**  
- Pull both knees deeper  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in cool-down or mobility  

## Benefits
- Lower back stretch  
- Glute release  
- Hip mobility  

## Tips for Success
- Relax shoulders  
- Breathe deeply  
- Avoid jerky motion
