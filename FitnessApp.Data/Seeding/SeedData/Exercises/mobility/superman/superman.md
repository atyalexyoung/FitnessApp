## Form Cues
1. Lie prone, arms extended forward.  
2. Lift arms, chest, and legs simultaneously.  
3. Hold, then lower with control.  

## Common Mistakes
- Overarching lower back  
- Rapid lifting  
- Neck strain  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lift arms or legs individually  
- Shorter hold  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Add small pulses  
- Slow tempo  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in posterior chain or mobility routines  

## Benefits
- Strengthens lower back, glutes, and shoulders  
- Improves thoracic extension  
- Core engagement  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly  
- Engage glutes and core  
- Avoid neck strain
