## Form Cues
1. Stand near wall or doorway.  
2. Place forearm on wall at 90° angle.  
3. Step forward to feel stretch across chest.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning torso excessively  
- Shoulder shrug  
- Overstretching elbow  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Step less forward  
- Hold shorter  

**Harder:**  
- Step further  
- Rotate slightly away from wall  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in cool-down or posture routine  

## Benefits
- Chest flexibility  
- Shoulder mobility  
- Posture improvement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep spine neutral  
- Relax shoulder  
- Breathe deeply
