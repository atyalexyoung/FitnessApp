## Form Cues
1. Sit with one leg bent in front, other leg bent behind.  
2. Rotate torso toward front leg, supporting with arm.  
3. Hold and breathe deeply.  

## Common Mistakes
- Hunching back  
- Twisting too forcefully  
- Knee or ankle strain  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Sit on cushion  
- Keep back leg straighter  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Add forward reach  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in cool-down or mobility  

## Benefits
- Glute stretch  
- Spinal rotation  
- Hip mobility  

## Tips for Success
- Move gently  
- Avoid pain  
- Focus on deep breathing
