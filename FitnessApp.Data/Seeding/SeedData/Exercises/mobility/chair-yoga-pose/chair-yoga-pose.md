## Form Cues
1. Stand with feet hip-width apart.  
2. Sit hips back as if sitting in a chair, arms forward.  
3. Keep spine tall, core engaged, knees over ankles.  

## Common Mistakes
- Heels lifting  
- Rounded lower back  
- Knees collapsing inward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shallower squat  
- Support arms on wall  

**Harder:**  
- Deeper squat  
- Hold longer  
- Add twist or arm extension  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s hold  
- Include in warm-up, yoga, or mobility sessions  

## Benefits
- Hip and ankle mobility  
- Quad and glute activation  
- Core and thoracic engagement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep heels down  
- Engage glutes and core  
- Move slowly
