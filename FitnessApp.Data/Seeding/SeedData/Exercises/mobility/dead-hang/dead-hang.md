## Form Cues
1. Hang from pull-up bar with shoulders relaxed.  
2. Keep grip firm, torso stable, and engage lats slightly.  
3. Hold position without swinging.  

## Common Mistakes
- Shoulder shrugging  
- Swinging  
- Bending elbows  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Feet on ground for partial hang  
- Shorter hold  

**Harder:**  
- Longer hold  
- One-arm hang progression  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 15–60s hold  
- Include in grip and shoulder mobility routines  

## Benefits
- Shoulder stability  
- Grip strength  
- Thoracic extension  

## Tips for Success
- Avoid swinging  
- Keep core engaged  
- Breathe steadily
