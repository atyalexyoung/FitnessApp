## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, cross ankle over opposite knee.  
2. Open and close crossed leg side to side like windshield wiper.  
3. Maintain upright torso and core engagement.  

## Common Mistakes
- Twisting spine  
- Using momentum  
- Collapsing hips  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller range  
- Hands support hips  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at end range  
- Slow tempo  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 8–12 reps per side  
- Include in hip mobility circuits  

## Benefits
- Glute and hip mobility  
- Improves external rotation  
- Supports squats and lunges  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Move controlled  
- Focus on glute stretch
