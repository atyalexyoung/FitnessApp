## Form Cues
1. Grab a pole with both hands, step back.  
2. Lean torso away from pole, keeping arms straight.  
3. Hold and breathe, feeling stretch along sides.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Bending elbows  
- Rotating hips  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Step less far back  
- Keep torso closer  

**Harder:**  
- Step further  
- Slight side bend  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in warm-up, cool-down, or mobility session  

## Benefits
- Lats and teres major stretch  
- Shoulder mobility  
- Upper-body lengthening  

## Tips for Success
- Keep arms straight  
- Avoid arching back  
- Move slowly
