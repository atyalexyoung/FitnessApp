## Form Cues
1. Sit or lie on back, knees bent.  
2. Rotate knees to one side, arms extended.  
3. Keep shoulders on ground, core engaged.  

## Common Mistakes
- Lifting shoulders  
- Rapid rotation  
- Hips tilting  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees less  
- Shorter hold  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Straighten legs slightly  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–30s per side  
- Include in cool-down  

## Benefits
- Thoracic and lumbar mobility  
- Glute and oblique stretch  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly  
- Maintain shoulder contact  
- Breathe deeply
