## Form Cues
1. Start on all fours.  
2. Thread one arm under the opposite arm, lowering shoulder toward floor.  
3. Rotate thoracic spine and hold, then return to start.  

## Common Mistakes
- Collapsing hips  
- Rotating lower back instead of thoracic spine  
- Rushing movement  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Reduce rotation  
- Support head with hand  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at end range  
- Add breathing hold  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 6–10 reps per side  
- Include in warm-up or mobility flow  

## Benefits
- Improves thoracic rotation  
- Shoulder and core mobility  
- Reduces upper back tension  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly  
- Keep core engaged  
- Focus on thoracic rotation
