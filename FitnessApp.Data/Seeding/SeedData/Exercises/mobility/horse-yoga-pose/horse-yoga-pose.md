## Form Cues
1. Stand with feet wide, toes slightly out.  
2. Squat down, keeping knees over toes, spine tall.  
3. Engage glutes and core, hold position.  

## Common Mistakes
- Knees collapsing inward  
- Leaning torso forward  
- Heels lifting  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Higher squat  
- Hands support balance  

**Harder:**  
- Deeper squat  
- Hold longer  
- Add side twists or arm lifts  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s hold  
- Include in warm-up, mobility, or yoga flow  

## Benefits
- Hip and groin mobility  
- Leg strength  
- Core stabilization  

## Tips for Success
- Keep spine tall  
- Engage glutes and core  
- Move controlled
