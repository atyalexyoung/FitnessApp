## Form Cues
1. Begin in Triangle Pose.  
2. Rotate torso, bringing bottom hand to opposite foot/shin, top arm toward ceiling.  
3. Keep chest tall and hips aligned.  

## Common Mistakes
- Collapsing torso  
- Over-twisting lower back  
- Locking knees  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter reach  
- Slight twist  

**Harder:**  
- Full extension  
- Hold longer  
- Slow controlled movement  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in yoga flows or mobility routines  

## Benefits
- Hip, hamstring, and spinal rotation  
- Shoulder mobility  
- Balance and stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips level  
- Engage core  
- Move controlled and slow
