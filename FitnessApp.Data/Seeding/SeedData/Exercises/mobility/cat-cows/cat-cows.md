## Form Cues
1. Start on all fours, shoulders over wrists, hips over knees.  
2. Inhale, drop belly, lift chest and tailbone (Cow).  
3. Exhale, round spine, tuck chin and pelvis (Cat).  
4. Flow smoothly, coordinating with breath.  

## Common Mistakes
- Moving too fast  
- Collapsing shoulders  
- Arching lower back excessively  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller spinal movement  
- Support wrists with padding  

**Harder:**  
- Hold end positions longer  
- Add shoulder protraction/retraction  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Include as warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Improves spinal mobility  
- Enhances posture and shoulder mobility  
- Engages core  

## Tips for Success
- Sync movement with breath  
- Keep shoulders away from ears  
- Move controlled and fluid
