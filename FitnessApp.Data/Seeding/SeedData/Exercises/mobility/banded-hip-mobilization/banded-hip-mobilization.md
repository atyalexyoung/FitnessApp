## Form Cues
1. Anchor band to stable point, loop around hip.  
2. Move hip in circles or forward/back while keeping core engaged.  
3. Control motion and avoid compensations.  

## Common Mistakes
- Overextending  
- Losing core stability  
- Fast, uncontrolled motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter band  
- Smaller circles  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier band  
- Longer hold  
- Add multi-plane motion  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 8–12 reps per side  
- Include in hip mobility warm-up  

## Benefits
- Hip joint mobility  
- Glute activation  
- Prepares for squats/lunges  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly  
- Engage core  
- Maintain tension in band
