## Form Cues
1. Stand with wide stance, arms extended horizontally.  
2. Hinge at hips, reach front hand to shin/foot, opposite hand up.  
3. Keep torso open and chest tall.  

## Common Mistakes
- Collapsing torso  
- Locking knees  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter reach  
- Bend front knee slightly  

**Harder:**  
- Longer hold  
- Deeper hinge  
- Rotate gaze upward  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in mobility or yoga flow  

## Benefits
- Hamstring and hip mobility  
- Shoulder and thoracic mobility  
- Improves balance  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest open  
- Align hips  
- Move controlled
