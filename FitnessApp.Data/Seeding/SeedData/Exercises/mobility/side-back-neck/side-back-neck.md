## Form Cues
1. Sit or stand tall.  
2. Tilt head to one side, then slightly back or rotate gently.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Shrugging shoulders  
- Rotating torso  
- Jerky motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller tilt  
- Support with hand  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Add slight rotation  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–30s per side  
- Include in warm-up, post-workout, or office mobility  

## Benefits
- Neck flexibility  
- Trap and rhomboid stretch  
- Reduces tension and stiffness  

## Tips for Success
- Keep shoulders relaxed  
- Move gently  
- Avoid over-rotation
