## Form Cues
1. Start in a lunge position, back knee slightly off ground.  
2. Place inside hand on floor, rotate thoracic spine, reach opposite arm up.  
3. Step forward and back to repeat.  
4. Maintain tall posture and engage core.  

## Common Mistakes
- Collapsing chest  
- Rushing through rotations  
- Dropping hips  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Keep back knee down  
- Shorter rotation  

**Harder:**  
- Add reach or overhead sweep  
- Hold longer at end range  
- Flow slowly with controlled breathing  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 6–8 reps per side  
- Include in warm-up, mobility, or yoga flow sessions  

## Benefits
- Opens hips, thoracic spine, shoulders  
- Improves rotation and hip mobility  
- Prepares body for dynamic movements  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly and with breath  
- Keep core engaged  
- Focus on controlled thoracic rotation
