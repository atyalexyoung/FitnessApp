## Form Cues
1. Start in deep squat, feet flat.  
2. Reach arms overhead while keeping chest tall.  
3. Hold, breathe, and return to start.  

## Common Mistakes
- Heels lifting  
- Rounding back  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Support with hands on block  
- Shallower squat  

**Harder:**  
- Deeper squat  
- Hold overhead longer  
- Add rotation  

## Programming Options
- 2–4 sets × 15–30s hold  
- Include in warm-up, mobility, or yoga flows  

## Benefits
- Improves hip, ankle, and thoracic mobility  
- Shoulder flexibility  
- Core stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep heels down  
- Engage core  
- Move controlled
