## Form Cues
1. Lie on foam roller across mid-back.  
2. Extend thoracic spine gently over roller.  
3. Keep chin tucked and core engaged.  

## Common Mistakes
- Overarching lumbar spine  
- Rapid movement  
- Neck strain  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use softer roller  
- Shorter extension  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Add arm reach overhead  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in warm-up or recovery  

## Benefits
- Improves thoracic extension  
- Enhances posture  
- Shoulder mobility  

## Tips for Success
- Move slow and controlled  
- Keep core engaged  
- Avoid lower back arch
