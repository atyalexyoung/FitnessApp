## Form Cues
1. Start on all fours, hands under shoulders, knees under hips.  
2. Extend opposite arm and leg fully, keeping torso stable.  
3. Hold, then return to start.  

## Common Mistakes
- Sagging hips  
- Rotating torso  
- Neck strain  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lift only arm or leg  
- Shorter hold  

**Harder:**  
- Hold longer  
- Add pulses  
- Slow tempo  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 8–12 reps per side  
- Include in core and mobility circuits  

## Benefits
- Core and glute stability  
- Thoracic spine control  
- Balance improvement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips level  
- Engage core  
- Move controlled
