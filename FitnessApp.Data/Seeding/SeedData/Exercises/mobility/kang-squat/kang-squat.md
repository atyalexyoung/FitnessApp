## Form Cues
1. Begin in deep squat with feet shoulder-width apart.  
2. Move torso forward slightly, arms extended.  
3. Hold and return to start with control.  

## Common Mistakes
- Heels lifting  
- Leaning too far forward  
- Collapsing chest  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Higher squat  
- Use wall for support  

**Harder:**  
- Lower squat  
- Hold longer  
- Add arm rotations  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–30s  
- Include in mobility or warm-up routines  

## Benefits
- Hip and ankle mobility  
- Core engagement  
- Thoracic extension  

## Tips for Success
- Keep heels down  
- Engage glutes and core  
- Move controlled
