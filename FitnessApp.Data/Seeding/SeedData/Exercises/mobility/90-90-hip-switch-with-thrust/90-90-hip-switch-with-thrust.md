## Form Cues
1. Perform standard 90/90 hip switch.  
2. As you switch, thrust hips slightly forward to deepen stretch.  
3. Maintain upright torso and controlled motion.  

## Common Mistakes
- Overarching lower back  
- Rushing the movement  
- Twisting torso  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller thrust  
- Hands support torso  

**Harder:**  
- Longer hold in end position  
- Deeper thrust  
- Slow and controlled  

## Programming Options
- 2–4 sets × 6–8 reps per side  
- Include in hip mobility or yoga flows  

## Benefits
- Deepens hip opening  
- Improves hip control  
- Supports squats, lunges, and rotational movements  

## Tips for Success
- Engage core  
- Move controlled  
- Focus on hip rotation and stretch
