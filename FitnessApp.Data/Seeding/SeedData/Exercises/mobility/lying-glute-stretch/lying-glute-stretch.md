## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, cross one ankle over opposite knee.  
2. Pull bottom leg toward chest until stretch is felt in glute.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Lifting hips off floor  
- Pulling leg too aggressively  
- Rounding spine  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Keep bottom leg bent slightly  
- Use strap around leg  

**Harder:**  
- Pull knee closer to chest  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Glute and piriformis stretch  
- Hip mobility  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips on floor  
- Breathe deeply  
- Relax shoulders
