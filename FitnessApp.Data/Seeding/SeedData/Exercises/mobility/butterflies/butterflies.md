## Form Cues
1. Sit with soles of feet together, knees out to sides.  
2. Hold feet with hands, keep back straight.  
3. Gently press knees toward the floor.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Bouncing knees  
- Pulling feet too aggressively  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Sit on folded towel  
- Bring heels further from body  

**Harder:**  
- Lean torso forward  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s hold  
- Include in warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Inner thigh flexibility  
- Hip mobility  

## Tips for Success
- Keep spine upright  
- Press knees gently  
- Breathe deeply
