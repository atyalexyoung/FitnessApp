## Form Cues
1. Step one foot forward into a lunge, back foot angled slightly out.  
2. Square hips forward, raise arms overhead.  
3. Maintain upright torso and engaged core.  

## Common Mistakes
- Hips not squared  
- Back leg knee collapsing  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter stance  
- Keep hands on hips  

**Harder:**  
- Longer hold  
- Deeper lunge  
- Add side bend  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s hold per side  
- Include in mobility or yoga flow  

## Benefits
- Hip flexor and quad stretch  
- Thoracic and shoulder mobility  
- Core stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips square  
- Engage glutes and core  
- Breathe steadily
