## Form Cues
1. Flow through poses such as Downward Dog, Warrior I/II, Cobra, Cat-Cow.  
2. Move smoothly from one pose to the next.  
3. Focus on controlled breathing.  

## Common Mistakes
- Holding breath  
- Rushing through poses  
- Collapsing joints  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter sequences  
- Use props (blocks, straps)  

**Harder:**  
- Longer hold times  
- Advanced transitions  
- Add balance challenges
