## Form Cues
1. Lie on back holding weight overhead with one arm.  
2. Roll to elbow, then hand, lift hips, sweep leg back, stand up.  
3. Reverse slowly to starting position.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Rushing transitions  
- Losing balance  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use lighter weight or no weight  
- Practice partial transitions  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier weight  
- Full slow controlled movement  
- Pause at each transition  

## Programming Options
- 2–4 sets × 3–6 reps per side  
- Include in mobility or strength routines  

## Benefits
- Full-body mobility  
- Core and shoulder stability  
- Improves coordination and balance  

## Tips for Success
- Move slowly and controlled  
- Keep weight overhead stable  
- Focus on posture and transitions
