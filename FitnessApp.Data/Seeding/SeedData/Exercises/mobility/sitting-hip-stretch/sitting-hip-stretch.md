## Form Cues
1. Sit with one ankle crossed over opposite knee.  
2. Lean torso slightly forward, keeping spine straight.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Pulling too aggressively  
- Shoulder tension  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Sit on cushion  
- Keep torso upright  

**Harder:**  
- Lean further forward  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per side  
- Include in cool-down or mobility  

## Benefits
- Glute and piriformis flexibility  
- Hip mobility  
- Relieves tension  

## Tips for Success
- Breathe slowly  
- Keep spine tall  
- Move gently
