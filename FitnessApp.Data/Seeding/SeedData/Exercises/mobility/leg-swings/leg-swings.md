## Form Cues
1. Stand on one leg, swing opposite leg forward/back.  
2. Keep torso upright, core engaged.  
3. Increase range gradually.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning torso  
- Swinging too fast  
- Not controlled  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Hold support  
- Shorter swing  

**Harder:**  
- Swing higher  
- Slow tempo  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 10–15 swings per leg  
- Include in warm-up  

## Benefits
- Hip mobility  
- Hamstring and glute activation  
- Prepares for dynamic movements  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core engaged  
- Control motion  
- Avoid bouncing
