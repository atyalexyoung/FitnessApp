## Form Cues
1. Sit or stand tall.  
2. Tilt head to one side, ear toward shoulder.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Shrugging shoulder  
- Twisting torso  
- Jerky motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Support head lightly with hand  
- Shorter hold  

**Harder:**  
- Apply gentle pressure  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–30s per side  
- Include in warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Neck flexibility  
- Reduces tension  
- Prepares for upper-body lifting  

## Tips for Success
- Keep shoulders down  
- Move slowly  
- Avoid pain
