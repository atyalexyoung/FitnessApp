## Form Cues
1. Stand tall, pull one ankle toward glutes.  
2. Keep knees together, torso upright.  
3. Hold and breathe.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Knees apart  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use wall for balance  
- Hold shorter  

**Harder:**  
- Pull ankle closer  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 20–40s per leg  
- Include in warm-up or cool-down  

## Benefits
- Quadriceps flexibility  
- Hip flexor stretch  
- Knee health  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips forward  
- Engage core  
- Breathe steadily
