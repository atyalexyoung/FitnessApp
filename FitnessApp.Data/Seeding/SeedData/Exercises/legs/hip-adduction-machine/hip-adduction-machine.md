## Form Cues
1. Sit on machine, pads against inner thighs.
2. Press legs together slowly.
3. Return under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Jerky movement  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Strengthens inner thighs  
- Hip stability  

## Tips for Success
- Smooth controlled motion  
- Full range
