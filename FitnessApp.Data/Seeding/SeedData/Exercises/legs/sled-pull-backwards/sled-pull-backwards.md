## Form Cues
1. Attach harness or rope, face sled.
2. Walk backward, keeping knees slightly bent.
3. Maintain controlled pace.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning too far  
- Overstriding  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter sled  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier sled  
- Longer distance  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 15–30m  

## Benefits
- Hamstring and glute development  
- Conditioning  

## Tips for Success
- Small steps  
- Keep torso upright
