## Form Cues
1. Place feet on leg press platform, toes forward.
2. Press through balls of feet to raise heels.
3. Lower slowly below platform level.

## Common Mistakes
- Locking knees  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Calf development  
- Strengthens plantar flexors  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Full range  
- Avoid bouncing
