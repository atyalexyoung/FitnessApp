## Form Cues
1. Stand in front of box, barbell on back.
2. Descend until glutes lightly touch box.
3. Drive through heels to stand.

## Common Mistakes
- Sitting too heavily  
- Rounding back  
- Knee collapse  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Higher box  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at box  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 5–10 reps  

## Benefits
- Squat depth consistency  
- Quad/glute/hamstring strength  
- Posterior chain activation  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled descent  
- Heels on floor  
- Core tight
