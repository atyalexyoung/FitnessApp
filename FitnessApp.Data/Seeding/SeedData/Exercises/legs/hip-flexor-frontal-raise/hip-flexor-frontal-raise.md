## Form Cues
1. Stand or sit, lift one leg straight forward.
2. Lower slowly under control.
3. Keep torso stable.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning back  
- Swinging leg  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Add ankle weight  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Hip flexor strength  
- Functional mobility  
- Quad activation  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Keep torso upright
