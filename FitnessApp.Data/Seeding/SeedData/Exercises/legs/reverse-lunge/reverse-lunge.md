## Form Cues
1. Step backward with one leg, lower until knee near floor.
2. Front knee over ankle, torso upright.
3. Push through front heel to return.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Short step  
- Knee passing toes  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Dumbbells  
- Weighted vest  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Quad and glute development  
- Posterior chain activation  
- Knee-friendly  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Step through heel  
- Keep torso upright
