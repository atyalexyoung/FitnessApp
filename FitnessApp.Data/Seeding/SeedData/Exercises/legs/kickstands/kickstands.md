## Form Cues
1. Stand on one leg, hinge at hips.
2. Extend free leg behind, lower torso forward.
3. Return upright, keeping balance.

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding back  
- Knee collapse  
- Losing balance  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  
- Hold support  

**Harder:**  
- Dumbbell  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Glute and hamstring development  
- Balance improvement  
- Posterior chain activation  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Hinge at hips  
- Controlled motion
