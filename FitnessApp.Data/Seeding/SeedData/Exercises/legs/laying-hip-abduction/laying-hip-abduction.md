## Form Cues
1. Lie on side, bottom leg bent, top leg straight.
2. Lift top leg upward, then lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Rolling hips backward  
- Jerky motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Add ankle weight  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Gluteus medius development  
- Hip stability  
- Balance  

## Tips for Success
- Keep hips stacked  
- Controlled motion
