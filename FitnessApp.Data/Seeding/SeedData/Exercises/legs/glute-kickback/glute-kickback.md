## Form Cues
1. Position on cable machine or bench.
2. Extend one leg behind, squeeze glutes.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Arching back  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Cable or machine resistance  
- Pause at contraction  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Glute isolation  
- Hip extension strength  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Squeeze glutes at top
