## Form Cues
1. Barbell on front delts, elbows high.
2. Descend by pushing hips back and knees forward.
3. Maintain upright torso, depth below parallel.
4. Drive through heels to stand.

## Common Mistakes
- Elbows dropping  
- Torso leaning forward  
- Heels lifting  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Goblet squat  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 5–10 reps  

## Benefits
- Quad-dominant  
- Core and upper back strength  
- Knee health and mobility  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows up  
- Chest tall  
- Controlled descent
