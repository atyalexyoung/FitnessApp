## Form Cues
1. Stand on platform or machine with heels hanging.
2. Raise heels as high as possible.
3. Lower slowly below level of platform.

## Common Mistakes
- Bouncing  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  
- Lower platform height  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Builds calf strength and size  
- Ankle stability  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Full range  
- Squeeze at top
