## Form Cues
1. Sit on leg extension machine, pad on shins.
2. Extend legs fully.
3. Lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Hyperextending knees  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Quad isolation  
- Knee extensor strength  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Avoid locking knees
