## Form Cues
1. Sit on machine, pads on outside of thighs.
2. Push legs apart slowly.
3. Return under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Jerky motion  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Strengthens glutes  
- Outer thigh development  
- Hip stability  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Full range
