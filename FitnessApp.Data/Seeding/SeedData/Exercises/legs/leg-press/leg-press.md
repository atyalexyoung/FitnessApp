## Form Cues
1. Sit on leg press machine, feet shoulder-width.
2. Lower platform until knees ~90°.
3. Press back up without locking knees.

## Common Mistakes
- Locking knees  
- Lifting hips off pad  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Builds leg strength  
- Safer alternative to squats for knees  
- Quad and glute development  

## Tips for Success
- Full range  
- Control movement  
- Avoid hip lift
