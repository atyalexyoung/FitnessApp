## Form Cues
1. Sit upright, knees bent, feet off floor.
2. Lift knees toward chest, lower slowly.
3. Keep torso stable.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning back  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower range  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Add ankle weights  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Strengthens hip flexors  
- Improves core stability  
- Functional lifting benefit  

## Tips for Success
- Keep torso upright  
- Controlled motion
