## Form Cues
1. Hold dumbbell/kettlebell at chest.
2. Descend by pushing hips back, knees over toes.
3. Lower until thighs ~parallel, then stand.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward  
- Letting knees cave in  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Quad-dominant  
- Glute development  
- Core stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chest up  
- Heels on floor  
- Controlled descent
