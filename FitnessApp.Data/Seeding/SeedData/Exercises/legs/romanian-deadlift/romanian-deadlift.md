## Form Cues
1. Hold barbell at hip level, feet hip-width.
2. Hinge at hips, lower bar along thighs.
3. Maintain slight bend in knees.
4. Stop when hamstrings fully stretched, return upright.

## Common Mistakes
- Rounding lower back  
- Locking knees  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Dumbbells instead of barbell  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 6–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Hamstring and glute development  
- Hip hinge pattern improvement  
- Posterior chain strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep back neutral  
- Hinge at hips, not waist  
- Controlled motion
