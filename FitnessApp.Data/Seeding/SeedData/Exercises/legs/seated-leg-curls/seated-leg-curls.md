## Form Cues
1. Sit on machine, pad on back of ankles.
2. Curl heels toward glutes.
3. Slowly return to start.

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Hamstring isolation  
- Improves posterior chain balance  

## Tips for Success
- Smooth motion  
- Full range
