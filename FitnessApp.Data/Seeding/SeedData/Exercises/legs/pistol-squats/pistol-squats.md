## Form Cues
1. Stand on one leg, other leg extended forward.
2. Descend slowly until thigh near parallel.
3. Press through heel to return upright.

## Common Mistakes
- Knee valgus  
- Leaning forward  
- Not controlling descent  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Hold onto support  
- Partial range  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted pistol  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 5–10 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Single-leg strength  
- Quads and glutes  
- Balance and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Full range  
- Controlled descent  
- Keep torso upright
