## Form Cues
1. Stand inside trap bar, shoulders aligned with handles.
2. Descend into squat, keeping back neutral.
3. Drive through heels to stand.

## Common Mistakes
- Heels lifting  
- Rounding back  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 5–10 reps  

## Benefits
- Quad, glute, hamstring strength  
- Safer than back squat for spine  
- Posterior chain activation  

## Tips for Success
- Core tight  
- Controlled motion  
- Heels on floor
