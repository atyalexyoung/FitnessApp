## Form Cues
1. Attach ankle strap to cable, face down or standing.
2. Extend leg backward, squeeze glutes.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Arching back  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower weight  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Glute and hamstring activation  
- Hip extension strength  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Avoid torso sway
