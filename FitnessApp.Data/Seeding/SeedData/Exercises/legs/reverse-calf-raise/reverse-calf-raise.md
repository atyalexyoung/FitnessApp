## Form Cues
1. Stand on edge or flat surface, toes facing forward.
2. Lift toes upward while heels remain on ground.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Strengthens tibialis anterior  
- Improves ankle stability  

## Tips for Success
- Full range  
- Controlled motion
