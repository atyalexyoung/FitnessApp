## Form Cues
1. Step out to side, bend leading knee, keep trailing leg straight.
2. Keep torso upright, descend until thigh ~parallel.
3. Push through heel to return.

## Common Mistakes
- Knee caving in  
- Leaning forward  
- Short step  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  
- Shorter step  

**Harder:**  
- Dumbbells  
- Weighted vest  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Quad and glute development  
- Hip mobility  
- Lateral strength  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Full range  
- Keep torso upright
