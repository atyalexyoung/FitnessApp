## Form Cues
1. Load safety squat bar, stand shoulder-width.
2. Descend by pushing hips back, knees over toes.
3. Lower until thighs ~parallel, stand upright.

## Common Mistakes
- Heels lifting  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter bar  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 5–10 reps  

## Benefits
- Quad and glute hypertrophy  
- Upper back support  
- Safer for shoulders  

## Tips for Success
- Heels on floor  
- Chest up  
- Controlled depth
