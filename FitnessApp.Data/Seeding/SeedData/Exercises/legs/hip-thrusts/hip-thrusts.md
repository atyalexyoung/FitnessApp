## Form Cues
1. Sit with upper back on bench, barbell across hips.
2. Drive through heels, extend hips fully.
3. Lower under control without touching ground.

## Common Mistakes
- Overarching lower back  
- Not full hip extension  
- Feet too far or close  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  
- Single-leg hip thrust  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Glute hypertrophy  
- Posterior chain strength  
- Hip extension power  

## Tips for Success
- Keep chin tucked  
- Drive through heels  
- Controlled motion
