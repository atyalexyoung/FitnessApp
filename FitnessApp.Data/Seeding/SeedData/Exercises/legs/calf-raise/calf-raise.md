## Form Cues
1. Stand on platform with heels hanging, weight loaded.
2. Raise heels as high as possible.
3. Lower slowly below platform level.

## Common Mistakes
- Bouncing  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  
- Lighter load  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Gastrocnemius hypertrophy  
- Ankle stability  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Full range  
- Squeeze at top
