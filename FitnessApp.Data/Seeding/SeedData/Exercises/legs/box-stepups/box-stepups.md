## Form Cues
1. Step onto box with one foot, drive through heel to stand.
2. Lower under control.
3. Alternate legs.

## Common Mistakes
- Pushing off back leg  
- Leaning forward  
- Short step  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower box  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Dumbbells  
- Higher box  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Unilateral strength  
- Glute and quad development  
- Balance and coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Step through heel  
- Controlled motion  
- Keep torso upright
