## Form Cues
1. Lie face down on machine, pad behind ankles.
2. Curl heels toward glutes.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Hamstring isolation  
- Strengthens posterior chain  

## Tips for Success
- Smooth controlled reps  
- Avoid jerking
