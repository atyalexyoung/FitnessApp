## Form Cues
1. Step one foot forward, back foot on ground or elevated.
2. Lower until front thigh ~parallel.
3. Push through front heel to stand.

## Common Mistakes
- Knee caving in  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Dumbbells  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Quad and glute development  
- Balance and stability  
- Unilateral strength  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled descent  
- Keep torso upright
