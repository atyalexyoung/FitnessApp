## Form Cues
1. Stand on elevated surface, one foot hanging off edge.
2. Slowly lower heel toward floor, controlling descent.
3. Return to start using other leg or assist.

## Common Mistakes
- Fast lowering  
- Hip drop  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lower height  
- Use support  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps per leg  

## Benefits
- Quad strengthening  
- Balance improvement  
- Knee stability  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled motion  
- Keep torso upright  
- Full range
