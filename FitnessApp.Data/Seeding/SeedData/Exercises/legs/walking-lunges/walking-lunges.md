## Form Cues
1. Step forward with one leg, lower until back knee almost touches floor.
2. Front knee over ankle, torso upright.
3. Push off front foot to step forward with opposite leg.

## Common Mistakes
- Knee passing toes  
- Leaning forward  
- Short steps  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Stationary lunges  
- Bodyweight only  

**Harder:**  
- Hold dumbbells  
- Weighted vest  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 steps per leg  

## Benefits
- Unilateral leg strength  
- Glute and quad development  
- Balance improvement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep torso upright  
- Step far enough  
- Controlled motion
