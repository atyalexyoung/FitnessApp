## Form Cues
1. Stand on hack squat machine, shoulders against pads.
2. Descend until knees ~90°, feet flat.
3. Press back up without locking knees.

## Common Mistakes
- Lifting heels  
- Partial range  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Quad dominant  
- Safer on knees  
- Glute development  

## Tips for Success
- Keep feet flat  
- Controlled depth
