## Form Cues
1. Stand on flat side of half medicine ball.  
2. Engage core and maintain balance, avoiding foot movement.  
3. Hold for desired duration.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning excessively  
- Locking knees  
- Looking down instead of forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Stand on floor with one foot on ball  
- Hold onto support  

**Harder:**  
- Close eyes  
- Lift one foot slightly  
- Hold weight  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–60 seconds  
- Include in warm-up or balance-focused sessions  

## Benefits
- Ankle stability  
- Core activation  
- Improves proprioception
