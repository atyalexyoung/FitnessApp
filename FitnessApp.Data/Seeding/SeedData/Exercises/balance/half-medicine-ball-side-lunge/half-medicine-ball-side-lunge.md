## Form Cues
1. Stand beside half medicine ball.  
2. Step one leg onto the ball, hinge at hips, lunge slightly.  
3. Keep core tight, balance, and return to start.  

## Common Mistakes
- Collapsing knee inward  
- Leaning torso too far  
- Hopping instead of controlled movement  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Step onto floor instead of ball  
- Hold onto support  

**Harder:**  
- Add dumbbell load  
- Pause on ball  
- Slow eccentric movement  

## Programming Options
- 2–3 sets × 8–12 reps per side  
- Include in balance or lower-body circuits  

## Benefits
- Improves lateral stability  
- Strengthens legs and glutes  
- Enhances proprioception
