## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, lift legs and shoulders off floor.  
2. Keep lower back pressed to floor.  
3. Arms extended overhead or alongside ears.  
4. Hold steady.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Dropping legs  
- Holding breath  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees  
- Arms at sides  

**Harder:**  
- Straight legs  
- Weighted arms or ball  
- Hold longer  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–60s  
- Include in core circuits or gymnastic training  

## Benefits
- Core stabilization  
- Ab endurance  
- Total abdominal activation  

## Tips for Success
- Engage abs and glutes  
- Control breathing  
- Maintain straight body line
