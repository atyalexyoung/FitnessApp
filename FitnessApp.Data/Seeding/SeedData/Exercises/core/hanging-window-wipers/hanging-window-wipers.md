## Form Cues
1. Hang from pull-up bar with arms extended.  
2. Lift legs straight and rotate side to side in controlled arc.  
3. Engage core throughout.  

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging body  
- Bent knees (less advanced)  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees  
- Shorter arc  

**Harder:**  
- Straight legs  
- Longer range  
- Weighted ankles  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 6–12 reps per side  
- Include in advanced core or gym circuits  

## Benefits
- Oblique and core strength  
- Rotational stability  
- Grip and lats activation  

## Tips for Success
- Engage core and lats  
- Move controlled  
- Avoid swinging
