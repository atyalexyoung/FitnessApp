## Form Cues
1. Lie flat on back, arms extended overhead.  
2. Simultaneously lift legs and upper body to form a V.  
3. Lower slowly without touching floor.  

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Arching lower back  
- Incomplete range of motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees  
- Reduce range  

**Harder:**  
- Hold weight overhead  
- Slow negatives  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in core circuits or HIIT  

## Benefits
- Upper and lower abdominal strength  
- Hip flexor engagement  
- Core stabilization  

## Tips for Success
- Move controlled  
- Keep core tight  
- Avoid neck strain
