## Form Cues
1. Stand tall holding dumbbell in one hand.  
2. Bend sideways at waist toward weight.  
3. Return to upright slowly and repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning forward or back  
- Using momentum  
- Too heavy weight  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Smaller bend  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier dumbbell  
- Slow negative  
- Pause at bottom  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps per side  
- Include in core or oblique circuits  

## Benefits
- Oblique strengthening  
- Core stability  
- Weighted lateral movement  

## Tips for Success
- Keep torso straight  
- Move controlled  
- Avoid twisting forward/back
