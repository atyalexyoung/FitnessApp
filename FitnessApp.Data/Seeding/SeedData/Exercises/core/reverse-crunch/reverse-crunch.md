## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, arms at sides, knees bent 90°.  
2. Curl hips toward chest while keeping upper back on floor.  
3. Lower slowly and repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging legs  
- Using momentum  
- Arching back  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Keep knees bent  
- Smaller lift  

**Harder:**  
- Straight-leg reverse crunch  
- Add pause at top  
- Weighted ankles  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in core circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower ab activation  
- Core stabilization  
- Minimal hip flexor strain  

## Tips for Success
- Engage abs  
- Move controlled  
- Keep back pressed to floor
