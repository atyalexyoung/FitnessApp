## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, hands behind head, legs lifted.  
2. Bring elbow toward opposite knee while extending other leg.  
3. Alternate sides rhythmically.  

## Common Mistakes
- Pulling on neck  
- Fast uncontrolled reps  
- Legs too low or too high  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Keep feet on floor  
- Slower pace  

**Harder:**  
- Hold pause at elbow-to-knee  
- Weighted crunch  
- Faster intervals  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 15–20 reps per side  
- Include in core or HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Abdominal and oblique strengthening  
- Core endurance  
- Rotational stability  

## Tips for Success
- Engage abs, not neck  
- Controlled twisting  
- Keep shoulders lifted
