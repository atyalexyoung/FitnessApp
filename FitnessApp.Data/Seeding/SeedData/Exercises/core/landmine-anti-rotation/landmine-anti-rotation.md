## Form Cues
1. Hold landmine with both hands at chest.  
2. Step to side and resist rotation as bar wants to twist torso.  
3. Keep core tight and stable throughout.  

## Common Mistakes
- Allowing torso to twist  
- Using momentum  
- Leaning forward  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Smaller stance  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier bar  
- Longer hold  
- Add slow rotation pause  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–12 reps  
- Include in core or anti-rotation circuits  

## Benefits
- Core stabilization  
- Oblique strengthening  
- Anti-rotation core activation  

## Tips for Success
- Keep torso rigid  
- Move controlled  
- Engage core fully
