## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, hold medicine ball overhead.  
2. Explosively throw ball backward or overhead, engaging core.  
3. Catch ball under control and repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching back  
- Using arms only  
- Uncontrolled landing  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter medicine ball  
- Smaller throw distance  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier medicine ball  
- Slow negatives
- Explosive throw overhead or backward  
- Add pause at catch  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Include in core circuits or explosive training  

## Benefits
- Core power and stability  
- Oblique and abdominal engagement  
- Shoulder and lat activation  
- Improves coordination and control  

## Tips for Success
- Maintain tight core throughout  
- Avoid arching lower back  
- Control catch and throw  
- Start with lighter medicine ball, progress gradually
