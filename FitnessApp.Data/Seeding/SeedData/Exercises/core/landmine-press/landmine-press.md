## Form Cues
1. Hold end of landmine with one hand.  
2. Press bar overhead while maintaining core stability.  
3. Avoid torso rotation during lift.  

## Common Mistakes
- Twisting torso  
- Overarching back  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use lighter weight  
- Seated press  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier weight  
- Single-arm press standing  
- Slow negative phase  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 8–12 reps per side  
- Include in core/shoulder circuits  

## Benefits
- Shoulder and core strengthening  
- Anti-rotation core activation  
- Improves stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Move controlled  
- Avoid torso rotation
