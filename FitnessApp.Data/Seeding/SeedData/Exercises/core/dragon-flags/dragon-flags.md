## Form Cues
1. Lie on bench, hold edges behind head.  
2. Lift entire body, keeping torso and legs straight.  
3. Lower slowly without touching bench, maintain tension.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Using momentum  
- Incomplete control  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Partial range  
- Bend knees  

**Harder:**  
- Full straight-leg  
- Slow negatives  
- Add holds at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 5–10 reps  
- Include in advanced core or gymnastic circuits  

## Benefits
- Full core activation  
- Total body tension  
- Advanced abdominal strength  

## Tips for Success
- Maintain tension throughout  
- Controlled motion  
- Avoid arching
