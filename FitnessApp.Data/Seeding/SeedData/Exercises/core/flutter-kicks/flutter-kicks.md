## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, legs extended, hands under glutes.  
2. Lift legs slightly off ground, alternate up and down.  
3. Maintain core engagement throughout.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Legs too high  
- Moving too fast  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees slightly  
- Reduce speed  

**Harder:**  
- Straight legs  
- Longer intervals  
- Weighted ankles  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 20–40s  
- Include in core or HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower ab activation  
- Hip flexor strength  
- Core endurance  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Legs just above floor  
- Controlled tempo
