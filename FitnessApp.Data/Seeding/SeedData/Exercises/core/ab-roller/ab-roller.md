## Form Cues
1. Kneel on floor, hold ab roller.  
2. Roll forward, keeping core tight and back neutral.  
3. Roll back to starting position under control.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Using momentum  
- Too fast rolling  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Roll out shorter distance  
- Use wall stop  

**Harder:**  
- Straight-leg rollout  
- Pause at full extension  
- Weighted vest  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Include in core or advanced circuits  

## Benefits
- Full core activation  
- Anti-extension strength  
- Improves shoulder and hip stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core engaged  
- Move controlled  
- Avoid back arching
