## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, arms up, knees bent 90°.  
2. Extend opposite arm and leg toward floor.  
3. Return to start and alternate sides.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Moving too fast  
- Losing control  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Keep legs higher  
- Smaller range of motion  

**Harder:**  
- Slow tempo  
- Add resistance bands  
- Hold positions longer  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–12 reps per side  
- Include in core or mobility circuits  

## Benefits
- Core stabilization  
- Coordination between limbs  
- Anti-extension strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Control movement  
- Avoid back arching
