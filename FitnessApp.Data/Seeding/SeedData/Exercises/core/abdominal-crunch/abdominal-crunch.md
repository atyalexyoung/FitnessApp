## Form Cues
1. Lie on your back, knees bent, feet flat.  
2. Place hands behind head or across chest.  
3. Engage core and lift shoulder blades off floor.  
4. Lower under control and repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Pulling neck with hands  
- Using momentum  
- Not engaging core  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Hands across chest  
- Partial range of motion  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted crunch  
- Stability ball crunch  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 12–20 reps  
- Include in core circuits  

## Benefits
- Rectus abdominis strengthening  
- Core activation  
- Improves posture  

## Tips for Success
- Focus on controlled movement  
- Avoid neck strain  
- Keep lower back on floor
