## Form Cues
1. Sit on edge of bench or chair, hold sides for support.  
2. Extend legs straight, then raise them toward chest.  
3. Lower under control, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging legs  
- Leaning back too far  
- Not engaging core  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees  
- Smaller range  

**Harder:**  
- Hold weight between feet  
- Straight-leg holds  
- Slow negatives  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in core circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower abdominals activation  
- Hip flexor engagement  
- Core endurance  

## Tips for Success
- Keep back straight  
- Engage abs  
- Control motion
