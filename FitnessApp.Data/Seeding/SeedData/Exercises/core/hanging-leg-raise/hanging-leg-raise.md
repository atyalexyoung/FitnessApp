## Form Cues
1. Hang from pull-up bar with arms extended.  
2. Engage core and lift legs straight or bent to 90°.  
3. Lower slowly without swinging.  

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging body  
- Using momentum  
- Incomplete range of motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bent knees  
- Hanging knee raises  

**Harder:**  
- Straight-leg raise  
- Weighted ankle cuffs  
- Toes-to-bar  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 8–15 reps  
- Include in core or HIIT circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower abdominals and hip flexor strength  
- Core stabilization  
- Grip strength (from hanging)  

## Tips for Success
- Control movement  
- Avoid swinging  
- Engage core throughout
