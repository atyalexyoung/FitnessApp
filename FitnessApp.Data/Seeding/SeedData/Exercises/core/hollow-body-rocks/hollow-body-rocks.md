## Form Cues
1. Start in hollow body hold position.  
2. Rock gently forward and backward from shoulders to hips.  
3. Maintain core tightness and back pressed to floor.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Using momentum  
- Breathing irregularly  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter rocking range  
- Bend knees  

**Harder:**  
- Straight legs  
- Increase rocking amplitude  
- Weighted arms  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–20 rocks  
- Include in core or gymnastics circuits  

## Benefits
- Core engagement  
- Ab endurance  
- Coordination and control  

## Tips for Success
- Engage core throughout  
- Control movement  
- Keep spine neutral
