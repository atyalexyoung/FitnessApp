## Form Cues
1. Hold end of landmine with both hands, arms extended.  
2. Rotate torso side to side in controlled motion.  
3. Keep hips stable and core engaged.  

## Common Mistakes
- Twisting hips  
- Using momentum  
- Rounding spine  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter bar  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier bar  
- Slow negatives  
- Longer range  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps per side  
- Include in core or oblique circuits  

## Benefits
- Oblique and rotational strength  
- Core stability  
- Weighted rotational training  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Move controlled  
- Avoid twisting lower back
