## Form Cues
1. Lie on floor, feet hooked to sled or resistance.  
2. Lift legs toward torso, keeping core tight.  
3. Lower slowly under control.  

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging legs  
- Arching back  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Bend knees  

**Harder:**  
- Straight legs  
- Pause at top  
- Add slow negatives  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in core or weighted circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower abdominal strengthening  
- Hip flexor engagement  
- Core stabilization  

## Tips for Success
- Engage abs  
- Move controlled  
- Keep back pressed down
