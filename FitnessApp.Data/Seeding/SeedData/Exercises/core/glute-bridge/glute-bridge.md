## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat.  
2. Engage glutes and lift hips to form straight line.  
3. Lower slowly without touching ground, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Overarching lower back  
- Uneven hips  
- Not engaging glutes  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Partial lift  
- Shorter hold  

**Harder:**  
- Single-leg bridge  
- Weighted bridge  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 12–20 reps  
- Include in core or glute circuits  

## Benefits
- Glute and hamstring strengthening  
- Lower back support  
- Core activation  

## Tips for Success
- Engage glutes and abs  
- Keep line straight  
- Control movement
