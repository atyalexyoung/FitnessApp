## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, knees bent, feet anchored.  
2. Sit up and rotate torso to one side at top.  
3. Lower under control and alternate sides.  

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Pulling neck  
- Fast uncontrolled movement  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Partial rotation  
- Hands across chest  

**Harder:**  
- Hold weight  
- Full twist at top  
- Slow negatives  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 12–20 reps per side  
- Include in core circuits  

## Benefits
- Oblique and rectus abdominis strengthening  
- Core endurance  
- Rotational stability  

## Tips for Success
- Move controlled  
- Engage core  
- Avoid neck strain
