## Form Cues
1. Lie on back, arms up, knees bent 90°.  
2. Extend opposite arm and leg toward floor.  
3. Alternate sides, keeping back pressed to floor.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Moving too quickly  
- Losing control  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smaller arm/leg movements  
- Keep knees higher  

**Harder:**  
- Slow tempo  
- Add resistance bands  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–12 reps per side  
- Include in core circuits or mobility work  

## Benefits
- Core stability  
- Anti-extension strength  
- Limb coordination  

## Tips for Success
- Engage abs  
- Maintain neutral spine  
- Move slowly and controlled
