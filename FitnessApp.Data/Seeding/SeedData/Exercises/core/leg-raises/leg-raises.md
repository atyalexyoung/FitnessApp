## Form Cues
1. Lie flat on your back, arms at sides or under glutes.  
2. Lift legs straight up to 90° slowly.  
3. Lower legs slowly without touching floor.  

## Common Mistakes
- Arching lower back  
- Using momentum  
- Not engaging core  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees  
- Reduce range of motion  

**Harder:**  
- Add ankle weights  
- Slow negative phase  
- Hanging leg raises  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in core circuits or HIIT  

## Benefits
- Lower ab strength  
- Hip flexor engagement  
- Core stabilization  

## Tips for Success
- Engage abs throughout  
- Keep lower back pressed into floor  
- Control movement
