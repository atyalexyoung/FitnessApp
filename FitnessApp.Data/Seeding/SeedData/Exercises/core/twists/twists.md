## Form Cues
1. Sit, stand, or kneel depending on variation.  
2. Hold weight if using, rotate torso side to side.  
3. Engage core and control movement.  

## Common Mistakes
- Using arms only  
- Fast uncontrolled movement  
- Leaning forward or back  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- No weight  
- Smaller rotation  

**Harder:**  
- Add dumbbell, cable, or medicine ball  
- Extend arms  
- Slow negatives  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps per side  
- Include in core or circuit training  

## Benefits
- Oblique and rotational core strength  
- Core endurance  
- Postural stabilization  

## Tips for Success
- Engage obliques  
- Move controlled  
- Keep torso upright
