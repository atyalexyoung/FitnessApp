## Form Cues
1. Sit on bench or chair, hold edges.  
2. Extend legs straight and lift toward chest.  
3. Lower slowly under control, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging legs  
- Leaning back  
- Not engaging abs  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bend knees  
- Smaller lift  

**Harder:**  
- Hold dumbbell between feet  
- Straight-leg pause at top  
- Increase reps  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in core circuits  

## Benefits
- Lower abdominal activation  
- Hip flexor strength  
- Core stabilization  

## Tips for Success
- Engage core  
- Keep back straight  
- Move slowly and controlled
