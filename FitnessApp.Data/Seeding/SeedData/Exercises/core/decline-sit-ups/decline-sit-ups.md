## Form Cues
1. Sit on decline bench, feet anchored.  
2. Cross arms over chest or behind head.  
3. Crunch up while engaging abs.  
4. Lower slowly, repeat.  

## Common Mistakes
- Pulling neck  
- Using momentum  
- Not engaging abs  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Shorter range  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted plate  
- Pause at top  
- Slow negatives  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 12–20 reps  
- Include in core circuits  

## Benefits
- Abdominal strengthening  
- Core endurance  
- Oblique engagement with rotation  

## Tips for Success
- Move controlled  
- Engage core  
- Avoid neck strain
