## Form Cues
1. Use underhand grip slightly narrower than usual.
2. Keep wrists straight and elbows close.
3. Lower bar to upper chest.
4. Press upward while maintaining wrist stability.

## Common Mistakes
- **Loose Grip:** Risk of slipping — use thumb around bar.
- **Flaring Elbows:** Keep tucked close.
- **Bar Path Too Low:** Should hit upper chest, not mid.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smith machine reverse press  

**Harder:**  
- Paused reps  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 6–10 reps  
- Substitute for incline pressing occasionally  

## Benefits
- Activates upper chest differently  
- Reduces shoulder stress  
- Increases triceps involvement  

## Tips for Success
- Use spotter for safety  
- Grip firmly and keep bar balanced
