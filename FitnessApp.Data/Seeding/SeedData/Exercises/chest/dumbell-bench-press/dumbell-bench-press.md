## Form Cues
1. Sit on bench holding dumbbells on thighs.
2. Lie back, kick weights into position above chest.
3. Lower dumbbells until elbows reach about 90°.
4. Press up, keeping dumbbells slightly angled toward each other.
5. Keep shoulders pinned and core braced.

## Common Mistakes
- **Touching Dumbbells Together at Top:** Reduces tension.
- **Dropping Elbows Too Low:** Overstretches shoulders.
- **Uneven Extension:** Focus on balanced press.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Neutral grip dumbbell press  
- Floor press  

**Harder:**  
- Tempo reps  
- Alternating-arm press  
- Add resistance bands  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Add as accessory after barbell bench  
- Superset with dumbbell flys  

## Benefits
- Enhances stability and coordination  
- Builds balanced chest development  
- Increases range of motion  

## Tips for Success
- Keep wrists neutral  
- Maintain even tempo  
- Focus on controlled stretch at bottom
