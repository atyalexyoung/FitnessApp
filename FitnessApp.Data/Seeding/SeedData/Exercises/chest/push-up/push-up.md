## Form Cues
1. Hands slightly wider than shoulders.
2. Engage core, keep straight line head-to-heels.
3. Lower chest close to floor.
4. Push back up without flaring elbows.

## Common Mistakes
- **Sagging Hips:** Tighten core to stay aligned.
- **Elbows Out:** Keep 45° angle.
- **Partial Reps:** Full range for max benefit.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Knee push-ups  
- Elevated surface push-ups  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted push-ups  
- Deficit push-ups  
- Clapping or explosive push-ups  

## Programming Options
- **Hypertrophy:** 4 sets × AMRAP  
- **Endurance:** Max reps in 2 min  
- **HIIT:** 30 sec on / 30 sec rest  

## Benefits
- Builds functional upper body strength  
- Improves shoulder stability  
- Engages core for stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep head neutral  
- Don’t flare elbows  
- Focus on controlled tempo
