## Form Cues
1. Sit upright with back flat.
2. Adjust handles to align with mid-chest.
3. Bring arms together in front, squeeze chest.
4. Slowly return until slight stretch is felt.

## Common Mistakes
- **Elbows Too Low or High:** Adjust arm pads to mid-chest height.
- **Bouncing at End Range:** Control tempo.
- **Shrugging Shoulders:** Keep relaxed and down.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric phase  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  
- Superset with presses  

## Benefits
- Great isolation for chest fibers  
- Safe controlled movement pattern  

## Tips for Success
- Focus on contraction, not speed  
- Keep constant tension
