## Form Cues
1. Set cables slightly below shoulder height.
2. Step forward, handles at chest level.
3. Press forward and slightly upward.
4. Control return until elbows just behind torso.

## Common Mistakes
- **Rounding Shoulders:** Keep chest up and open.
- **Uneven Cable Path:** Match both sides evenly.
- **Overextending Arms:** Maintain soft elbows.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use lighter weight  
- Seated cable press  

**Harder:**  
- Unilateral press  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Add near end of chest workout  

## Benefits
- Constant resistance through motion  
- Safer joint angles  
- Good for muscle control work  

## Tips for Success
- Keep stable stance  
- Focus on chest engagement  
- Maintain full range of motion
