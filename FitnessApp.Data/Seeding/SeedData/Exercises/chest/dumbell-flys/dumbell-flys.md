## Form Cues
1. Lie flat on a bench with dumbbells over chest.
2. Keep slight bend in elbows.
3. Lower arms in an arc until chest stretch is felt.
4. Bring dumbbells back up over chest with control.

## Common Mistakes
- **Locking Elbows:** Puts strain on joints.
- **Too Heavy Weight:** Reduces range and control.
- **Short Range of Motion:** Misses stretch benefit.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Cable flys  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Tempo flys  
- Deficit range on incline  

## Programming Options
- 3 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Superset with bench press  

## Benefits
- Improves chest shape and definition  
- Enhances flexibility and contraction strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows soft  
- Move slow through stretch  
- Focus on pec squeeze at top
