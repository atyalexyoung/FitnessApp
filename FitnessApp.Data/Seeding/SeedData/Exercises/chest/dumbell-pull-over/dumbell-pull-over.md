## Form Cues
1. Lie across bench perpendicular or fully flat.
2. Hold one dumbbell with both hands over chest.
3. Lower weight back in an arc behind head.
4. Pull back over chest using chest and lats.

## Common Mistakes
- **Dropping Too Deep:** Stresses shoulders.
- **Bending Arms Too Much:** Turns into triceps movement.
- **Rushing Motion:** Misses stretch benefit.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use light weight  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Use decline bench  
- Add pause at bottom  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  
- Use as finisher for chest or back days  

## Benefits
- Expands chest and ribcage mobility  
- Works both chest and lats  
- Improves breathing and posture  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Slow and controlled movement  
- Focus on stretch and contraction
