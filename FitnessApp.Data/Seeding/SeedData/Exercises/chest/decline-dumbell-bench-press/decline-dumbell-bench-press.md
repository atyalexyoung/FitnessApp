## Form Cues
1. Secure feet under pads, dumbbells on thighs.
2. Lie back, press weights over lower chest.
3. Lower dumbbells until elbows reach 90°.
4. Press up, keeping dumbbells under control.

## Common Mistakes
- **Losing Balance:** Secure core and grip.
- **Touching Dumbbells:** Keep slight separation.
- **Fast Eccentric:** Control descent.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Use machine decline press  

**Harder:**  
- Tempo reps  
- Alternating dumbbell press  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Use as accessory lower-chest work  

## Benefits
- Hits lower pecs directly  
- Improves pressing coordination  
- Allows natural arm path  

## Tips for Success
- Keep motion controlled  
- Focus on equal drive from both arms
