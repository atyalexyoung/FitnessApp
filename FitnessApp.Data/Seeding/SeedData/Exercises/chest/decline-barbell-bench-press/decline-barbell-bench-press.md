## Form Cues
1. Lie on decline bench with feet secured.
2. Grip bar slightly wider than shoulders.
3. Lower to lower chest/upper abs.
4. Press bar back up under control.

## Common Mistakes
- **Bar Path Too High:** Reduces lower chest focus.
- **Bouncing Off Chest:** Always controlled motion.
- **Loose Leg Drive:** Keep legs tight and anchored.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smith machine decline press  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Tempo descent  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 6–10 reps  
- Rotate with flat or incline pressing  

## Benefits
- Strengthens lower pecs  
- Reduces shoulder strain  
- Adds variety to pressing angles  

## Tips for Success
- Maintain full control  
- Don’t overarch back  
- Keep elbows slightly tucked
