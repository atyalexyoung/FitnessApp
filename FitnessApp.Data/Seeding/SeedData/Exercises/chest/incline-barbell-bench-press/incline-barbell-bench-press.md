## Form Cues
1. Set bench between 30–45°.
2. Grip bar slightly wider than shoulders.
3. Unrack bar, lower to upper chest.
4. Press upward and slightly back.

## Common Mistakes
- **Too High Incline:** Shifts load to shoulders.
- **Bar Path Too Straight:** Should move diagonally.
- **Losing Shoulder Retraction:** Keep upper back tight.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Smith machine incline press  

**Harder:**  
- Pause reps  
- Slow eccentric tempo  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 6–10 reps  
- Use in upper-chest hypertrophy day  

## Benefits
- Builds upper pec mass  
- Improves overhead strength transition  
- Reinforces pressing stability  

## Tips for Success
- Don’t lock out completely  
- Keep shoulder blades retracted  
- Focus on bar path consistency
