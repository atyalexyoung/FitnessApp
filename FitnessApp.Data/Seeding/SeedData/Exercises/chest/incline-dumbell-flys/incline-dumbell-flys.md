## Form Cues
1. Set bench at 30–45°.
2. Hold dumbbells above chest with elbows slightly bent.
3. Lower arms in an arc until stretch in upper pecs.
4. Bring weights back together over chest.

## Common Mistakes
- **Overstretching:** Can strain shoulders.
- **Using Too Heavy Dumbbells:** Lose control easily.
- **Short Motion:** Misses contraction at top.

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Flat flys  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric tempo  
- Incline cable flys  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Include in upper chest hypertrophy work  

## Benefits
- Builds upper chest detail  
- Improves muscle activation awareness  

## Tips for Success
- Keep steady control  
- Squeeze pecs at top  
- Avoid bouncing at bottom
