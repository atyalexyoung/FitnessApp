## Form Cues
1. Bend forward at hips with dumbbells or cable.
2. Pull weights outward, elbows slightly bent.
3. Squeeze rear delts at contraction.
4. Lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Rounding back  
- Using too heavy weight  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Seated incline bench  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at contraction  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  
- Rear delt focus  

## Benefits
- Improves posture and shoulder balance  
- Strengthens rear delts and traps  

## Tips for Success
- Slight elbow bend  
- Control motion  
- Avoid momentum
