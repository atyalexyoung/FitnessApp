## Form Cues
1. Stand side-on to cable, handle in outside hand.
2. Raise arm to shoulder height with slight elbow bend.
3. Lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging body  
- Lifting too high  
- Shrugging shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Medial delt focus  

## Benefits
- Builds shoulder width  
- Constant tension improves activation  

## Tips for Success
- Slight bend in elbows  
- Controlled motion  
- Avoid shrugging
