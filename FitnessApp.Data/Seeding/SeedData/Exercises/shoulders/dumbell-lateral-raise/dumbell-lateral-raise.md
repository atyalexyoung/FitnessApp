## Form Cues
1. Stand with dumbbells at sides, palms facing inward.
2. Raise arms to shoulder height with slight elbow bend.
3. Lower slowly, control the motion.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging dumbbells  
- Raising too high  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Seated version  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Focus on medial delt isolation  

## Benefits
- Builds shoulder width  
- Improves deltoid definition  

## Tips for Success
- Keep slight bend in elbows  
- Avoid shrugging shoulders  
- Use controlled motion
