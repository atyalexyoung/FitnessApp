## Form Cues
1. Stand feet shoulder-width apart, barbell at upper chest.
2. Grip just outside shoulders, elbows slightly forward.
3. Press bar overhead, fully extending elbows.
4. Lower slowly to chest, maintain upright posture.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning back excessively  
- Flaring elbows out  
- Using leg drive if strict press intended  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Dumbbell overhead press  
- Seated press  

**Harder:**  
- Heavy barbell press  
- Tempo pressing  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 5–12 reps  
- Primary shoulder mass builder  

## Benefits
- Builds deltoid size and strength  
- Strengthens triceps and upper traps  
- Functional overhead pressing strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core braced  
- Lead with elbows, not hands  
- Avoid arching lower back excessively
