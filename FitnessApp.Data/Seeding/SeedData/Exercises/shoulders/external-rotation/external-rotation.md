## Form Cues
1. Hold dumbbell or band, elbow bent 90°, tucked to side.
2. Rotate forearm outward, keep elbow fixed.
3. Slowly return to start.

## Common Mistakes
- Moving elbow away from body  
- Using too heavy weight  
- Swinging  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight or band  
- Seated  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at end  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3 sets × 12–15 reps  
- Shoulder rehab or warm-up  

## Benefits
- Strengthens rotator cuff  
- Improves shoulder stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbow tucked  
- Controlled motion  
- Avoid momentum
