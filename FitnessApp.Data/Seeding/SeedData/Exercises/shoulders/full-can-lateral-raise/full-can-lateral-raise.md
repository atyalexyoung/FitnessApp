## Form Cues
1. Hold dumbbells with thumbs up, at sides.
2. Raise arms to shoulder height, slight elbow bend.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Overextending  
- Shrugging shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Seated  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Medial delt emphasis  

## Benefits
- Builds deltoid width  
- Safer on shoulder joints  

## Tips for Success
- Control every rep  
- Slight elbow bend  
- Avoid momentum
