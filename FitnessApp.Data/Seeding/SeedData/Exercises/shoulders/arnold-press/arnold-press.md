## Form Cues
1. Sit or stand, hold dumbbells at chest, palms facing body.
2. Rotate wrists outward while pressing overhead.
3. Fully extend elbows at top.
4. Lower under control, rotate wrists back to start.

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Incomplete rotation  
- Flaring elbows too wide  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  
- Seated version  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Heavy dumbbells  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Target anterior delts specifically  

## Benefits
- Builds anterior shoulder mass  
- Improves shoulder mobility  
- Strengthens triceps  

## Tips for Success
- Control rotation  
- Avoid leaning back  
- Keep elbows under wrists during press
