## Form Cues
1. Stand facing away from low pulley, handle in hand.
2. Raise arm forward to shoulder height.
3. Lower slowly, maintain posture.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Using torso momentum  
- Overextending  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Anterior delt focus  

## Benefits
- Consistent tension  
- Builds front delts efficiently  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Lead with elbows  
- Avoid jerky motions
