## Form Cues
1. Sit or stand, dumbbells at shoulders.
2. Press overhead, fully extending elbows.
3. Lower under control to starting position.

## Common Mistakes
- Leaning back excessively  
- Flaring elbows  
- Uneven arm movement  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  
- Seated press  

**Harder:**  
- Heavy dumbbells  
- Tempo variations  

## Programming Options
- 3–5 sets × 6–12 reps  
- Primary shoulder strength builder  

## Benefits
- Builds deltoid and tricep strength  
- Improves overhead pressing stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep core tight  
- Press evenly  
- Avoid arching lower back
