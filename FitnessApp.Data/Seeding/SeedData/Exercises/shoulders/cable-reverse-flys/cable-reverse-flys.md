## Form Cues
1. Stand facing cable machine, arms crossing in front.
2. Pull handles apart to side, elbows slightly bent.
3. Squeeze rear delts, slowly return.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Rounded back  
- Too heavy  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Seated  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  
- Rear delt isolation  

## Benefits
- Strengthens rear delts and upper back  
- Improves posture  

## Tips for Success
- Slight elbow bend  
- Controlled motion  
- Avoid momentum
