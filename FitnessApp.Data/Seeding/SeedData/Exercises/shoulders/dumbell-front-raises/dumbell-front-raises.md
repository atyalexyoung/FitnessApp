## Form Cues
1. Stand, dumbbells in front of thighs.
2. Raise to shoulder height, palms down.
3. Lower slowly under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Swinging torso  
- Raising above shoulder height  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  
- Alternating arms  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Focus on anterior delt isolation  

## Benefits
- Builds front delts  
- Improves shoulder strength and aesthetics  

## Tips for Success
- Keep torso stable  
- Control every rep  
- Avoid swinging
