## Form Cues
1. Hold band or dumbbell, elbow bent 90°, tucked to side.
2. Rotate forearm inward toward body.
3. Slowly return.

## Common Mistakes
- Moving elbow away from body  
- Swinging  
- Too heavy resistance  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter band  
- Seated  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3 sets × 12–15 reps  
- Rotator cuff strengthening  

## Benefits
- Strengthens internal rotators  
- Enhances shoulder stability  
- Injury prevention  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbow tucked  
- Controlled motion  
- Avoid momentum
