## Form Cues
1. Hold barbell or dumbbells in front, palms facing body.
2. Pull to collarbone level, elbows leading.
3. Lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Pulling too high  
- Using momentum  
- Shrugging shoulders  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  
- Cable upright row  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  
- Trap and medial delt development  

## Benefits
- Builds traps and medial delts  
- Improves upper body strength  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbows  
- Maintain controlled motion  
- Avoid jerky movements
