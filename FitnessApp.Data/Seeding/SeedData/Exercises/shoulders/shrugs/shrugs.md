## Form Cues
1. Hold dumbbells or barbell at sides/front.
2. Shrug shoulders as high as possible.
3. Lower slowly, maintain neutral spine.

## Common Mistakes
- Rolling shoulders  
- Using momentum  
- Leaning forward/backward
