## Form Cues
1. Bend forward at hips, dumbbells hanging.
2. Raise arms to sides until parallel with floor.
3. Control lowering back to start.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging weights  
- Rounding back  
- Using too heavy dumbbells  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Seated on incline bench  
- Lighter dumbbells  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  
- Focus on rear delt isolation  

## Benefits
- Improves posterior shoulder development  
- Enhances posture and shoulder stability  

## Tips for Success
- Slight bend in elbows  
- Lead with elbows  
- Avoid momentum
