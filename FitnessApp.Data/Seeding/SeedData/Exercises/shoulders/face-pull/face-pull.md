## Form Cues
1. Attach rope to high pulley, grab with neutral grip.
2. Pull rope toward forehead, elbows high.
3. Squeeze shoulder blades at contraction.
4. Slowly release to start.

## Common Mistakes
- Shrugging shoulders  
- Using momentum  
- Elbows too low  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  
- Great for posture and rear delt development  

## Benefits
- Strengthens rear delts and upper back  
- Improves posture  
- Reduces shoulder injury risk  

## Tips for Success
- Lead with elbows  
- Keep chest upright  
- Controlled movement
