## Form Cues
1. Hold grip trainer in hand.
2. Squeeze fully, hold briefly, release slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Jerky motion  
- Partial squeeze  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter gripper  

**Harder:**  
- Longer holds  
- Higher resistance  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 15–25 reps  

## Benefits
- Improves grip strength  
- Strengthens forearms  

## Tips for Success
- Controlled squeeze  
- Full range
