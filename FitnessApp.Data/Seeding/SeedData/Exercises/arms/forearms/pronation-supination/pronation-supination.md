## Form Cues
1. Hold dumbbell vertically, elbow at 90° at side.
2. Rotate wrist so palm faces up (supination), then down (pronation).
3. Move slowly and controlled.

## Common Mistakes
- Moving elbow  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbell  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at rotation extremes  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Strengthens forearm rotators  
- Improves wrist stability  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbow tucked  
- Controlled rotation
