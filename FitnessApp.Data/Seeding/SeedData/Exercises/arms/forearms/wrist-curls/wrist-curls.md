## Form Cues
1. Sit, forearms on thighs, palms up holding weight.
2. Curl wrists upward, lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Moving elbows  
- Partial range  
- Swinging  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Dumbbells  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Strengthens forearm flexors  
- Improves grip  

## Tips for Success
- Forearms fixed  
- Controlled motion
