## Form Cues
1. Sit on bench, forearms resting on thighs, palms down holding barbell/dumbbells.
2. Curl wrists upward, then lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Moving elbows  
- Using momentum  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Use dumbbells  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Strengthens forearm extensors  
- Improves grip strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep forearms stationary  
- Controlled motion
