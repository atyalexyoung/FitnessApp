## Form Cues
1. Hold rope with both hands or one hand.
2. Perform twisting, pulling, or rolling motions to target forearm muscles.
3. Controlled movement throughout.

## Common Mistakes
- Using momentum  
- Incomplete range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter rope or slower pace  

**Harder:**  
- Heavier rope  
- Fast controlled motions  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–20 reps  

## Benefits
- Builds grip and forearm strength  
- Improves functional pulling motions  

## Tips for Success
- Smooth, controlled motions  
- Full range of movement
