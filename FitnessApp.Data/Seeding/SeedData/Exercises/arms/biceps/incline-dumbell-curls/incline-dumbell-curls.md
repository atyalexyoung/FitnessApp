## Form Cues
1. Sit on incline bench, arms hanging straight down.
2. Curl dumbbells toward shoulders.
3. Lower fully under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Using torso momentum  
- Swinging  
- Partial reps  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  
- Shorter range  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Biceps long head isolation  
- Improves peak and shape  

## Tips for Success
- Keep upper arms stationary  
- Control every rep
