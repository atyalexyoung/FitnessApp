## Form Cues
1. Hold barbell or dumbbells, palms facing down.
2. Curl toward shoulders, elbows pinned.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Builds forearms and biceps  
- Strengthens grip  

## Tips for Success
- Elbows pinned  
- Smooth motion
