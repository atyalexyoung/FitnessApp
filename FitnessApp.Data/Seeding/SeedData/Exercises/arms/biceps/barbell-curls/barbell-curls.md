## Form Cues
1. Stand holding barbell, palms up.
2. Curl barbell toward chest.
3. Lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging bar  
- Partial reps  
- Moving elbows  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- EZ bar  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Builds biceps mass  
- Improves curling strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Controlled movement
