## Form Cues
1. Sit at preacher bench, barbell or dumbbells in hands.
2. Curl weight toward shoulders.
3. Lower fully under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Lifting shoulders  
- Partial reps  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  
- EZ bar  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Isolates biceps  
- Prevents cheating  
- Improves lower bicep peak  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows on pad  
- Smooth motion
