## Form Cues
1. Sit, elbow on inner thigh, dumbbell in hand.
2. Curl dumbbell toward shoulder.
3. Lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Moving elbow  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbell  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Peak biceps contraction  
- Isolation  

## Tips for Success
- Elbow fixed  
- Controlled motion
