## Form Cues
1. Stand facing low pulley, rope attachment.
2. Neutral grip, curl upward keeping elbows pinned.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Flaring elbows  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Brachialis and forearm development  
- Constant tension  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Controlled motion
