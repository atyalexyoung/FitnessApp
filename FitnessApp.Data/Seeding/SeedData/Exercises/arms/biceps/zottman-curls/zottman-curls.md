## Form Cues
1. Curl dumbbells with palms up.
2. Rotate wrists at top, lower palms down.
3. Control descent.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Skipping rotation  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Trains biceps and forearms  
- Improves grip strength  

## Tips for Success
- Full rotation  
- Smooth motion
