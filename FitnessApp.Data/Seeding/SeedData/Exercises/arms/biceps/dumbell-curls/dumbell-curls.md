## Form Cues
1. Stand, dumbbells at sides, palms forward.
2. Curl dumbbells to shoulders, elbows stationary.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging arms  
- Partial range  
- Moving elbows  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  
- Alternating curls  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Builds biceps mass  
- Improves curling strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Controlled motion
