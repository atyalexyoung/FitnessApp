## Form Cues
1. Lie face down on incline bench, arms hanging.
2. Curl dumbbells/bar toward shoulders.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Biceps isolation  
- Prevents cheating  

## Tips for Success
- Keep upper arms stationary  
- Controlled motion
