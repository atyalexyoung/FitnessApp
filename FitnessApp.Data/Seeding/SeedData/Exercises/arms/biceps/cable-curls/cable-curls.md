## Form Cues
1. Stand facing low cable, handle in hand.
2. Curl toward shoulders, elbows pinned.
3. Slowly return.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Alternating arms  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Maintains tension on biceps  
- Builds size and definition  

## Tips for Success
- Elbows pinned  
- Full range of motion  
- Controlled reps
