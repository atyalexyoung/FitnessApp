## Form Cues
1. Hold dumbbells at sides, palms facing inward.
2. Curl dumbbells toward shoulders keeping palms facing each other.
3. Lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging elbows  
- Partial range  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter dumbbells  
- Seated version  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Strengthens biceps and forearms  
- Improves arm thickness  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Controlled motion  
- Full range of motion
