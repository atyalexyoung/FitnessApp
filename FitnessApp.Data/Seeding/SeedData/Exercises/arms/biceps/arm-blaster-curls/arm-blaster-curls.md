## Form Cues
1. Place arm blaster around neck, barbell in hands.
2. Keep elbows pinned to blaster.
3. Curl bar to chest, lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Lifting elbows off blaster  
- Swinging bar  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter barbell  
- Dumbbell arm blaster curls  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Isolates biceps  
- Improves peak contraction  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Full range of motion
