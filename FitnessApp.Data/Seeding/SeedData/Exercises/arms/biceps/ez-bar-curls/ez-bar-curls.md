## Form Cues
1. Stand holding EZ bar, angled grip palms up.
2. Curl toward chest.
3. Lower slowly.

## Common Mistakes
- Elbows moving forward  
- Swinging  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter EZ bar  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at top  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Biceps mass  
- Less wrist strain  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Smooth motion
