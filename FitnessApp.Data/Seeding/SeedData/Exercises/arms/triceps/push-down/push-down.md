## Form Cues
1. Stand at cable with bar or rope attachment at high pulley.
2. Elbows tucked to sides, forearms vertical.
3. Push attachment down until elbows fully extended.
4. Slowly return to start.

## Common Mistakes
- Flaring elbows out  
- Using body momentum  
- Partial range of motion  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Single-arm cable  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at contraction  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  
- Tricep isolation  

## Benefits
- Builds triceps size and strength  
- Improves pushing strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Controlled motion  
- Full range of motion
