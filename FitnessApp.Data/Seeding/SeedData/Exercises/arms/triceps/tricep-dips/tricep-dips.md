## Form Cues
1. Position hands on parallel bars or bench.
2. Lower body until elbows ~90°.
3. Press back up until arms extended.

## Common Mistakes
- Flaring elbows  
- Leaning too far forward  
- Swinging body  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Bench dips with feet on floor  
- Assisted dips  

**Harder:**  
- Weighted dips  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Builds triceps strength and mass  
- Engages chest and front delts  
- Bodyweight-friendly  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows close to torso  
- Controlled movement  
- Avoid jerking
