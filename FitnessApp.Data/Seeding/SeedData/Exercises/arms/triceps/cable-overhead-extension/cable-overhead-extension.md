## Form Cues
1. Stand facing away from low pulley, rope behind head.
2. Elbows tucked, forearms vertical.
3. Extend arms overhead until elbows straight.
4. Return under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Flaring elbows  
- Using momentum  
- Partial range  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Single-arm cable  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at top  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 10–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Triceps long head isolation  
- Full range contraction  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Control every rep
