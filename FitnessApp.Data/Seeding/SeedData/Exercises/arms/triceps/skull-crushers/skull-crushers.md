## Form Cues
1. Lie on bench, hold barbell/dumbbells above chest.
2. Lower to forehead or behind head.
3. Extend arms back to starting position.

## Common Mistakes
- Elbows flaring  
- Using momentum  
- Dropping too fast  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- EZ bar  
- Dumbbells  

**Harder:**  
- Slow eccentric  
- Pause at bottom  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Builds triceps mass  
- Improves arm strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows pinned  
- Controlled motion
