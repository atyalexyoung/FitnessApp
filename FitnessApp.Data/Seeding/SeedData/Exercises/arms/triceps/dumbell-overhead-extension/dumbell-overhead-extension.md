## Form Cues
1. Sit or stand, hold dumbbell overhead with both hands.
2. Lower behind head, elbows tucked.
3. Press dumbbell back up until elbows straight.

## Common Mistakes
- Flaring elbows  
- Using momentum  
- Arching lower back  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Single-arm dumbbell  
- Lighter weight  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at bottom  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 8–12 reps  

## Benefits
- Long head triceps isolation  
- Improves pressing strength  

## Tips for Success
- Keep elbows close  
- Control motion  
- Avoid arching back
