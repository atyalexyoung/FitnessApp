## Form Cues
1. Hinge at hips, dumbbell in hand, elbow bent.
2. Extend arm backward until fully straight.
3. Lower under control.

## Common Mistakes
- Swinging dumbbell  
- Flaring elbow  
- Using momentum  

## Variations
**Easier:**  
- Lighter weight  
- Seated incline support  

**Harder:**  
- Pause at extension  
- Slow eccentric  

## Programming Options
- 3–4 sets × 12–15 reps  

## Benefits
- Triceps isolation  
- Improves muscle definition  

## Tips for Success
- Keep upper arm still  
- Controlled motion  
- Avoid swinging
